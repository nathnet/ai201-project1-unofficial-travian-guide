# Special Servers - 18 Cropper

> Source: Travian: Legends Support  
> URL: https://support.travian.com/en/articles/30-special-servers-18-cropper

---

On some special servers, the **18 Cropper Village** feature is enabled.
If this feature is active, it will always be **announced before the server starts**.

---

## Overview

This feature allows the existence of special **crop-only villages**, offering a huge advantage for crop production compared to regular 15- or 9-croppers.

There are **four 18-cropper villages** in total — one in each map quadrant:

1. (25 | 25)
2. (−25 | 25)
3. (25 | −25)
4. (−25 | −25)

Each of these villages has **150% crop oases** within reach, making them the most efficient crop producers on the map.

---

**Tip:** Because there are only four 18-croppers per world, expect **intense competition** for them — coordinate with your alliance early if you plan to claim one!
