# Hero Left-Hand Items

> Source: Travian: Legends Support  
> URL: https://support.travian.com/en/articles/92-hero-left-hand-items

---

Left-hand hero items provide a range of bonuses, including increased fighting strength, faster movement, reduced cranny protection, and faster troop travel between villages or alliance members. Each item comes in **three tiers**, with higher tiers granting stronger bonuses.

---

## **Shields**

Shields increase your hero’s **fighting strength**.

- **Small Shield** – +250 fighting strength
- **Shield** – +1000 fighting strength
- **Large Shield** – +4000 fighting strength

Higher-tier shields provide significantly greater combat bonuses.

---

## **Horns**

Horns increase the attack power of your **hero and all troops attacking with the hero** when fighting **Natars**.

- **Small Horn of the Natarian** – +20% attack vs. Natars
- **Horn of the Natarian** – +25% attack vs. Natars
- **Great Horn of the Natarian** – +30% attack vs. Natars

---

## **Bags (Thief Bags)**

Thief Bags reduce the **enemy cranny capacity**, allowing you to steal more resources during raids.
For Teuton heroes, these bonuses stack with their tribe bonus.

- **Pouch of the Thief** – 10% cranny reduction
- **Bag of the Thief** – 15% cranny reduction
- **Sack of the Thief** – 20% cranny reduction

---

## **Maps**

Maps increase your hero’s **return speed to the village** after completing an adventure or mission.

- **Small Map** – 30% faster return
- **Map** – 40% faster return
- **Large Map** – 50% faster return

---

## **Pennants**

Pennants increase troop movement speed **between your own villages** when the hero travels with the army.

- **Small Pennant** – 30% faster
- **Pennant** – 40% faster
- **Great Pennant** – 50% faster

---

## **Standards**

Standards increase troop movement speed **between alliance or confederation members**, again only when the hero is traveling with the troops.

- **Small Standard** – 15% faster
- **Standard** – 20% faster
- **Great Standard** – 25% faster
