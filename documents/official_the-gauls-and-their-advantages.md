# The Gauls and Their Advantages

> Source: Travian: Legends Support  
> URL: https://support.travian.com/en/articles/6-the-gauls-and-their-advantages

---

### Overview

The **Gauls** are the most peace-loving and balanced of all the tribes. Their troops are **highly trained in defensive tactics**, making them strong defenders and difficult targets for raiders.

However, their **legendary cavalry speed** also allows skilled players to surprise opponents with swift counterattacks and raids. This tribe offers **flexible gameplay** — capable of both offense and defense, teamwork or solo play, infantry or cavalry strategies.

> The Gauls are an excellent choice for **beginners**, thanks to their strong defenses and **natural protection against raids**.

---

### Playstyle Tips

- Ideal for **new players** who want to learn both defense and offense safely.
- Focus early on **defensive building upgrades** (like the Palisade) to deter attacks.
- Use the **Trapper** to capture enemy troops and protect your resources.
- Take advantage of your **speed bonus** to strike or reinforce faster than any other tribe.
- When expanding, build on your **cheap settlers** and balanced economy to grow quickly.

---

### Special Features

| **Speed Bonus** | Gaul troops are the **fastest units in the game**. |
| --- | --- |
| **Palisade** | Provides a **moderate defense bonus**, stronger than the Teutons’ wall but weaker than the Romans’. |
| **Merchants** | Carry **750 resources** at a speed of **24 fields/hour** — the fastest traders in the game. |
| **Cranny Size** | Crannies protect **1.5× more resources** from raids compared to other tribes. [Hiding Resources and & Cranny](https://support.travian.com/en/support/solutions/articles/7000068298) |
| **Siege Weapons** | **More expensive** than those of other tribes. |
| **Settlers** | **Cheaper to train**, allowing for faster expansion. |
| **Chieftain** | Lowers enemy village **loyalty by 20–25%**. |
| **Special Building – Trapper** | Captures enemy troops attacking your village. Excellent for **early defense**. [Trapper](https://support.travian.com/en/support/solutions/articles/7000065346) |
| **Hero Bonus** | Gains **+5 speed when mounted** (scaled with gameworld speed). |
