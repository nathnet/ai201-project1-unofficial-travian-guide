# Travian Plus / Gold Features / Gold Club

> Source: Travian: Legends Support  
> URL: https://support.travian.com/en/articles/126-travian-plus-gold-features-gold-club

---

In *Travian: Legends*, you can use **Gold** to unlock premium features that improve convenience and efficiency. Some features last for a limited time, while others remain active for the entire server.

---

### Travian Plus

**Travian Plus** provides helpful tools and interface improvements.
It is active for a set number of days and can be renewed anytime using Gold.

**Main Plus Features:**

- [Building and Research Waiting Loop](https://support.travian.com/en/articles/34-building-and-research-waiting-loop) – Queue multiple building and research tasks.
- [Larger Map](https://support.travian.com/articles/130) – View a wider area of your surroundings.
- Map highlights – Your own villages, alliance and federation members, and enemy villages are automatically marked.
- [Incoming and outgoing troop movements](https://support.travian.com/articles/135) – View all troop activity directly on the map.
- [Central Village Overview](https://support.travian.com/articles/42) – Manage key aspects of your village in one place.
- [User-defined links](https://support.travian.com/articles/134) – Create shortcuts to your preferred game pages.
- [Incoming attack warning in village list](https://support.travian.com/articles/70) – Get notified of incoming attacks in your village list.
- [Quick access buttons](https://support.travian.com/support/solutions/articles/7000062848) – Instantly open the market, barracks, stable, and workshop.
- [Merchant run twice](https://support.travian.com/articles/131) – Each merchant can complete two trips per order.
- [Research queue for smithy](https://support.travian.com/articles/34) – Queue multiple troop upgrades.

---

### Gold Features

These **Gold features** are available at any time and do not require a Plus or Gold Club membership.

**Available Options:**

- [Production bonus](https://support.travian.com/articles/129) – Boost your resource production.
- [Finish constructions and research orders](https://support.travian.com/articles/36) – Complete construction or research instantly.
- [NPC Merchant](https://support.travian.com/articles/38) – Instantly exchange resources within your village.
- [Gold and Silver Exchange](https://support.travian.com/articles/115) – Convert Gold into Silver or vice versa.
- [Master Builder](https://support.travian.com/articles/37) – Queue constructions even when resources are missing.

---

### Gold Club

The **Gold Club** costs **200 Gold** and remains active for the entire duration of a game world.
Once unlocked, its features can be used freely without limits.

> **Note:** Gold spent on the Gold Club is **non-refundable**, even after account deletion or server end.

**Gold Club Features:**

- [Archive reports and messages](https://support.travian.com/articles/59) – Keep your messages and reports organized.
- [Crop finder](https://support.travian.com/articles/53) – Locate high-crop fields easily.
- [Farm list](https://support.travian.com/articles/69) – Send multiple raids efficiently.
- [Troop Evasion](https://support.travian.com/articles/15) – Protect troops by sending them away before a hit.
- [Merchants run three times](https://support.travian.com/articles/132) – Each merchant can complete up to three trips.
- [Trade routes](https://support.travian.com/articles/60) – Automate regular resource deliveries.
