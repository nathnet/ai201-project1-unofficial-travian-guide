# Changing password after the ban

> Source: Travian: Legends Support  
> URL: https://support.travian.com/en/articles/124-changing-password-after-the-ban

---

Keeping your account secure is essential — especially after a ban related to password sharing or third-party tools. This guide explains **when and why you should change your password**, and how to keep your avatar safe.

---

### Creating a Strong Password

For security, your password should be **unique and difficult to guess**.
We recommend using:

- A mix of **letters and numbers**
- Both **uppercase and lowercase** characters
- **Symbols** for added strength

> Passwords are **case-sensitive**, so “Hero123” and “hero123” are different.

---

### If You Were Banned for Scripts & Bots (Third-Party Tools)

If you entered your Travian credentials on an **external website or tool**, your data may have been used without your consent.
This often causes **bans for script or bot use**, because such tools access the game automatically.

**What to do:**

1. **Change your password immediately.**
2. Never enter your Travian username or password on non-Travian websites.

> These third-party sites can use your credentials to scan the map, find animals, or villages — and any such activity violates the rules.

Changing your password stops these tools from accessing your avatar again.

---

### If You Were Banned for Password Sharing

If your ban was related to **password sharing**, the first and most important step is to **change your password immediately**.
Never share it again, and keep it private to avoid future issues.

---

### Summary

- Always use a unique, strong password for every server.
- Never use external websites or tools that request your Travian login.
- Inform Travian Games Support Team if your dual changes accounts.
