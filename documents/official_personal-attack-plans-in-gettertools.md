# Personal Attack Plans in Gettertools

> Source: Travian: Legends Support  
> URL: https://support.travian.com/en/articles/162-personal-attack-plans-in-gettertools

---

Gettertools lets you plan your own coordinated attacks — fake and real — without waiting on an off-coord. Follow these quick steps to set it up and create a plan.

---

## Before you start (one-time setup)

1. Create a Gettertools account and select your Travian: Legends gameworld.
2. In settings, switch functionality to **Full** so you unlock planning tools.
3. Set the **timezone** (server time or your local time—pick the one you’ll actually use).
4. If your village benefits from an artefact that affects **troop speed**, add it to that village in Gettertools so timings are accurate.
5. Enable **My personal plan** to reveal the planning menu.
(Page 1–2 show these steps, including the “Full” toggle and timezone/artefact fields.)

---

## Add targets

You can build a plan two ways. (Page 3 has both flows.)

### A) One by one (precise control)

- Choose **Start village → Target village → Speed & waves → Send/arrival time → Save**.
Use this when you need exact handling per target (e.g., different wave counts).

### B) Rapid mode (batch add—fastest)

- Set your **attacking village, number of waves, speed, and time** once.
- Select multiple **targets** at once and add them in a batch.
- Mark the **real** target first (usually shown red), then add **fakes** (blue).
- You can edit any entry afterward.
This is ideal for “mirroring” fake and real hits so they **look similar** under Eyes and **land together**.

---

## Practical tips

- Plan in **server time** if your alliance coordinates that way; otherwise stick to your own timezone to avoid mistakes.
- Include artefact effects (and Tournament Square level) so **travel times** are correct.
- Save a template in Rapid mode for future waves; just swap targets on new ops.

---

## Safety note (external tool)

- Never share your Travian password. You only need village/coord data to plan. ([Gettertools](https://www.gettertools.com/en/) is external to Travian.)
