# Truce

> Source: Travian: Legends Support  
> URL: https://support.travian.com/en/articles/62-truce

---

### What is a Truce?

A **truce** is a temporary break in the game, usually scheduled for **major international holidays** such as **New Year**. It’s meant to give players time to relax and spend time away from the game with friends and family.

---

### Truce Period and Announcements

Before each truce, the **start and end times** are officially announced.
The announcement may also include details on **when the “no starvation” period ends**.

*Example:*
During a truce and for **24 hours after it**, **troop starvation is disabled** — no troops die even if you run out of crop.

---

### Game Rules During Truce

Here’s what you can and cannot do while a truce is active:

#### You Can:

- **Complete hero adventures.**
- **Settle new villages.**
- **Scout** other villages — scouting remains possible.

#### You Cannot:

- **Attack** other players.

	- Any attacks landing during the truce will only **submit greetings**.
- **Attack Natar villages** or **oases**.

	- These daily tasks will be **automatically prefilled**, so you can still reach **100 points**.

---

### Important Notes

- Avoid sending attacks scheduled **too close** to the start or end of a truce — slight delays may occur when the truce begins or ends.
- **Natars will still attack** if you settle in the **grey area**.
- At both the **start** and **end** of a truce, it’s recommended to **log in and check your avatar** to prevent crop-related issues.
- If needed, **send and recall a single troop** to trigger a new **crop calculation** to ensure your troops remain safe
