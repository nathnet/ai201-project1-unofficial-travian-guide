# Vikings in Travian: Legends

> Source: Travian: Legends Support  
> URL: https://support.travian.com/en/articles/139-vikings-in-travian-legends

---

The Vikings are fierce warriors, explorers, and conquerors — bringing their own unique structures, hero abilities, and unit traits to *Travian: Northern Legends*. This guide covers everything you need to know about their **special abilities, buildings, hero features, and army composition.**

---

### Viking Special Abilities and Buildings

#### Viking Harbor

The Viking Harbor serves as the heart of their expansion — enabling faster and more efficient raids across the seas.

- Viking ships travel **24 fields per hour through deep water** (on x1 worlds), faster than the **18 fields per hour** of other tribes.
- This speed bonus applies to **raids only** (not attacks or reinforcements).
- Construction costs and other details are identical to regular harbors.

---

#### The Barricade

The **Barricade** is the Vikings’ defensive wall — solid but not their strongest feature, as Vikings prefer attacking.

- **Base defense:** 6 points per level
- **Durability:** 3
- **Defensive bonus:** +1.5% per level
- **Building cost:** 110 Wood, 170 Clay, 70 Iron, 50 Crop

Even though the Vikings are primarily offensive, a few Barricade levels can provide valuable defense for key villages.

### Viking Hero

#### Hero Ability

The Viking hero is a powerful leader, capable of turning the tide in conquest.

- When leading **attacks or raids**, the hero inflicts **+5% loyalty damage** on enemy villages.
- This bonus can **increase by 5 points** with your own *great celebrations* or **decrease by 5 points** if the defender hosts one.
- Standard conquest rules still apply — including same alliance limits, NAPs, capital restrictions, and the need for a chief or Jarl to complete the takeover.

> The Viking hero’s loyalty damage applies **before** any administrator’s effect, but the hero **cannot** conquer a village alone (even at 0% loyalty).

### The Jarl

Jarls are Viking leaders and administrators who command with both wisdom and authority.
They are responsible for conquering new villages and expanding your empire.

- **Loyalty Reduction:** 15–30%
- **Research Cost:** 18,250 Wood / 13,320 Clay / 20,040 Iron / 16,480 Crop
- **Research Time:** 5:23:45

Their presence inspires troops and ensures conquered lands integrate smoothly into your kingdom.

---

### The Berserker’s Unique Ability: *Damage on Death*

Berserkers are deadly attackers who continue to harm enemies even in death.

**Ability:**
Each Berserker killed while attacking or raiding (not defending) kills **1 enemy unit** in return.

- Walls and Watchtower bonuses **do not reduce** this effect.
- The ability is calculated **after** the main battle results.
- Wounded units still go to the hospital normally.

**Formula:**
`Surviving defenders (each type) × (Killed Berserkers ÷ Total surviving defenders)`

**Example:**
If 100 Berserkers die while attacking, and 200 Slave Militias + 100 Druidriders survive:

- Total defenders: 300
- 100 ÷ 300 = 0.333

Effect:

- 200 × 0.333 = **67 Slave Militias die additionally**
- 100 × 0.333 = **33 Druidriders die additionally**

### Summary

The Vikings combine **speed, power, and ferocity**, making them a formidable tribe for aggressive players.

- **Fast raids** via their unique harbor system
- **Devastating offense** with Berserkers and their death damage
- **Powerful hero** capable of extra loyalty reduction
- **Strong economic logistics** through enhanced trade ships

While their defenses are modest, their offensive and naval advantages make the Vikings ideal for players who prefer fast-paced, high-impact gameplay.
