# Special Servers - Item Crafting

> Source: Travian: Legends Support  
> URL: https://support.travian.com/en/articles/22-special-servers-item-crafting

---

Item Crafting is a feature introduced in **Reign of Fire**, the 2025 Annual Special. This system allows you to upgrade, break down, and create equipment items using materials. All explanations below follow the original mechanics exactly.

---

## **Item Rarity and Materials**

All equipment items have a **rarity level**:

- **Common, Uncommon, Rare, Epic**

In addition, there is a new consumable item called **material**, which also comes in all four rarity levels.
Equipment items still have **quality tiers**, but **materials do not**.

Common, uncommon, and rare items **may** be found on adventures (rarity influences how late in the game they appear).
**Epic items cannot be found** in adventures — they can only be created via crafting.

- [Special Servers - Common, Uncommon, Rare and Epic Items](https://support.travian.com/articles/23)

---

## **Refine**

If you have **two identical items**, you can refine them:

- Both items are consumed.
- One material of the **same rarity** is also required.
- The resulting item keeps the **same type and quality**, but its **rarity increases by one level**.
- **Epic items cannot be refined further.**

This process has a **guaranteed result**.

---

## **Smelt**

Any equipment item can be smelted into materials:

- You receive **one material instantly**, or **two materials** if you wait for the smelting process to finish (or use Gold to speed it up).
- Materials received always match the **rarity of the smelted item**.
- Smelting time depends on the **server speed**.
- [Game versions and speed](https://support.travian.com/articles/20)

---

## **Forge**

Forging allows you to **create a new random item**:

- Requires **3 materials** of any rarity.
- Before confirming, you will see the **chance distribution** for the resulting rarity and quality.
- Higher rarity materials increase the chance of crafting higher rarity equipment.

### **Item Type Selection**

The item type is fully random:

- Equal chance for: **right-hand, left-hand, body, helmet, boots**.
- Within each category, every specific item also has an **equal chance** of appearing.

### **Reforge Option (Gold Feature)**

Instead of accepting the first crafted item, you may reforge:

1. First reforge shows a **second item**.
2. You may choose between the two, or reforge one of them again.
3. Each additional reforge costs **increasing amounts of Gold**.
4. You can always choose only **one final item** at the end.

Each reforge generates a new item **using the same random rules**.
