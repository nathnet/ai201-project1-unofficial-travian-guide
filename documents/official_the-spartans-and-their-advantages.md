# The Spartans and Their Advantages

> Source: Travian: Legends Support  
> URL: https://support.travian.com/en/articles/10-the-spartans-and-their-advantages

---

The **Spartans** are renowned for their **military-focused society**, where warfare is a way of life. From early childhood, Spartans are trained in combat and discipline — their entire culture revolves around strength, endurance, and unity on the battlefield.

While they can construct defensive walls, Spartans rely primarily on their **elite troops** for protection. Their soldiers are **powerful, efficient, and resilient**, making them exceptional in the **late game**, when resources and troop quality matter most.

Training Spartan units takes time and investment, but their **crop efficiency** and **combat strength** make them a formidable force once their armies are built.

> The Spartans are best suited for **experienced or patient players** who prefer a **long-term, strategic approach** and powerful endgame armies.

---

### Playstyle Tips

- Focus on **late-game development** — Spartan troops are expensive and slow to train, but incredibly strong once ready.
- Maintain **steady resource production** early on to support high training costs later.
- Use your **Asclepeion** to recover troops efficiently after battles.
- Combine offensive and defensive tactics — Spartans can adapt to both roles with proper planning.

---

### Special Features

| **Elite Troops** | Extremely strong units that take longer to train and cost more resources. |
| --- | --- |
| **Efficient Crop Usage** | Units are stronger than other tribes’ but consume the **same crop amount**. |
| **Defensive Wall** | Provides only a **minor defensive bonus** compared to other tribes. |
| **Merchants** | Can carry **500 resources** (speed: **14 fields/hour**). |
| **Ephor** | The Spartan administrator; lowers enemy village **loyalty by 20–25%**. |
| **Special Building – Asclepeion** | Functions like a **Hospital**, but can **restore 60% of troops lost in battle**. [Hospital and Asclepeion](https://support.travian.com/en/support/solutions/articles/7000065344) |
| **Hero Bonus** | Gains **+50% additional fighting strength** from **Spartan weapons**, which also **boost bonuses** to Spartan units. [Hero Right-Hand Items](https://support.travian.com/en/support/solutions/articles/7000068825) |

---

### Strategy Summary

**Strengths:** Very strong and efficient troops, powerful late-game potential, troop restoration advantage.
**Weaknesses:** Slow training times, expensive units, and weak defensive wall.

> If you enjoy building a **disciplined and unstoppable military force** that dominates the late game, the Spartans are your ideal choice.
