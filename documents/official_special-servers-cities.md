# Special Servers - Cities

> Source: Travian: Legends Support  
> URL: https://support.travian.com/en/articles/79-special-servers-cities

---

On Annual Special gameworlds, you can **upgrade a village into a city**. Cities provide important strategic advantages but also come with specific requirements and costs.

---

## **Requirements**

To convert a village into a city, you must have:

- A gameworld that **supports cities** (Annual Specials only)
- **Town Hall level 20**
- An **empty settlement slot**

	- This slot is normally used to settle or conquer a new village
	- Converting a village into a city uses this slot instead

You can choose between founding a new village or upgrading an existing one into a city.

---

## **City Features**

Upgrading a village to a city grants several advantages:

- **3 additional building slots**
- **Resource fields can be upgraded to level 12** (in non-capital cities)
- **Walls can be upgraded with Watchtowers**, providing extra defensive bonuses

	- [Special Servers - Watchtowers](https://support.travian.com/articles/78)
- **Egyptians** can build **Waterworks**, but **only in cities** (including if the capital itself is a city)

---

## **Upgrading to a City**

The upgrade takes time, depending on server speed:

- **x1 or x2 servers:** 24 hours
- **x3 servers:** 12 hours

As soon as the upgrade begins:

- The required **settlement slot becomes locked immediately**
- The upgrade progress is visible:

	- In your **player profile** (village list)
	- On the **map**, when hovering over the village

### **Special Case: Conquest During Upgrade**

- If **another player conquers your village**, the upgrade is **cancelled**
- If **you** conquer your own village using another village you own, the upgrade **continues**
- There is **no other way** to stop the upgrade once it has started

---

## **Conquering a City**

Cities can be conquered like normal villages. After conquest:

- A city uses **2 settlement slots**
- If you currently have **only 1 free slot**, the conquest still succeeds
- Your next settlement slot will be automatically used when you gain it
