# Trapper

> Source: Travian: Legends Support  
> URL: https://support.travian.com/en/articles/41-trapper

---

The **Trapper** is a **Gaul-only building** that can be constructed in any Gaul village. It protects the village by placing **hidden traps** that capture enemy troops. Captured troops cannot damage your village while imprisoned. Trapped troops **continue to consume crop** from the village they belong to. A single Trapper can hold up to **400 traps**.

---

## **Trapper Features**

- **Raids cannot free trapped troops.** Only a successful **normal attack** can free them.
- When enemy troops are freed by a successful attack, **all used traps are destroyed**.
- If the trapper owner **releases the captives**, all destroyed traps can be **repaired for free**.
- When troops are freed by an attack, **25% of the trapped troops die**.
- If the troop owner **disbands** their trapped units, those units **die in the traps** and do not return home.
- Releasing or disbanding trapped troops is done via the **Rally Point**.
- After upgrading one Trapper to **level 20**, you may build **additional Trappers** in the same village.

A table in the original article lists the full construction costs, build times, and maximum trap capacity for each level.
