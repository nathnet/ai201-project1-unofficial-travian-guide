# Game Secrets: A Handy Toolkit

> Source: Unofficial Travian  
> URL: https://unofficialtravian.com/2025/01/12/game-secrets-a-handy-toolkit/  
> Written on May 15, 2024

---

Travian: Legends can be complex in terms of everyday calculations, but with the right tools, it gets simpler.

![](../assets/30b6999177_duck.png)
Here’s a handy list of links to tools that’ll help you navigate the game better. From Gettertools, which gives you game stats and alliance history, to Travco, a tool for managing your gameplay, there’s something for everyone. Want to plan your troop merges or figure out the best way to develop your resources? Check out the merge calculator. Kirilloid calculator contains info on almost every aspect of the game, be it resource development or battle planning. And if you need help with your cropfields, there’s a tool for that too!. Happy gaming!

**So, what tools players currently use the most to play the game?**

![](../assets/979aadf0a2_TL-Text_Seperator_1_Shield.png)
##### **[Kirilloid calculator](http://travian.kirilloid.ru/distance.php#cc=0,0,3,4&srv=1.46&size=400&spd=7&art=1)**

- Building and units costs and upgrades
- Offense and defense development calculator
- Resource development calculator
- War simulator
- Plenty of tables, logger and much more

![](../assets/979aadf0a2_TL-Text_Seperator_1_Shield.png)
##### **[Gettertools](https://www.gettertools.com/en/)**

Provides various information and planning options:

- Gameworld statistics
- Players activity and alliance history
- Possibility to plan, create and participate in off-operations. More information about how to create personal plans you can find here: [Getter Personal plan](https://blog.travian.com/2023/12/game-secrets-personal-plan-in-gettertools/)
- Map overview of your alliance settling

![](../assets/979aadf0a2_TL-Text_Seperator_1_Shield.png)
##### **[Travco](https://www.travcotools.com/en/)**

Relatively new tool for individual and alliance management:

- Inactive search (shows players in your surrounding with no population growth)
- Report attack function with some possibility to analyze incoming attacks (requires to be registered as alliance)
- Defense planning function (allows to set a defense call, that is automatically closed when the needed number of defense have been reported)
- Resource push planning function (allows to set a resource push call, that is automatically closed when the needed number of resources have been reported).

![](../assets/979aadf0a2_TL-Text_Seperator_1_Shield.png)
##### **[Merge calculator by wishmaster 3](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1aSWQHRd-zJeo1mgpf6pKvNy_0qlXLxX6YCiWGsVmMJk/edit#gid=386161294)**

For the gameworlds with the troop merging and troop forwarding feature. Allows to calculate best options for merging troops for gold and resources.

![](../assets/979aadf0a2_TL-Text_Seperator_1_Shield.png)
##### [Cropper development](https://blog.travian.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/10/Travian-Cropper-development-calculator.html)

Gives clear guidance in what order develop your cropfields to get maximum return on investment in least time. Supports special worlds (with city feature) and regular gameworlds.

![](../assets/979aadf0a2_TL-Text_Seperator_1_Shield.png)
##### **[Timer](https://vclock.com/timer/#countdown)**

Not really specific Travian: Legends tool but might be useful to send farm on a regular basis. Can be set on repeat with the needed interval to make a signal.

![](../assets/979aadf0a2_TL-Text_Seperator_1_Shield.png)
##### **[Game Secrets Table of Contents](https://blog.travian.com/game-secrets-guides-table-of-contents/)**

One link to rule them all. All guides published in this blog with short descriptions, separated into categories.

![](../assets/979aadf0a2_TL-Text_Seperator_1_Shield.png)
![](../assets/3a64443f0d_WotW_Roman_cropped.png)
