# Special Servers - Keep Tribe on Conquest

> Source: Travian: Legends Support  
> URL: https://support.travian.com/en/articles/29-special-servers-keep-tribe-on-conquest

---

On gameworlds with **Keep Tribe on Conquest** enabled, conquering villages works differently than on regular servers.

---

## **Conquering a Village from Another Player**

If you conquer a village from an avatar of another tribe, the **village keeps its original tribe**.
Your avatar always remains the tribe you selected during registration.

---

## **Conquering a Natar Village**

If you conquer a Natar village, the village becomes part of the **tribe of the chief’s village**.

---

## **Settling a New Village**

When settling a new village, the new village will always **match the tribe of the settlers’ village**.

---

## **Hero Ability**

Your hero’s tribe-specific abilities **never change**.
They always remain based on the **tribe you chose at registration**, even if you control villages from different tribes.

- [Hero Overview Article](https://support.travian.com/articles/45)
- [Hero Items Article](https://support.travian.com/articles/89)

---

## **Teuton Brewery**

A brewery party started in a **Teuton capital** affects **all villages and troops on the avatar**, regardless of their tribe.

- [Brewery Article](https://support.travian.com/articles/76)
