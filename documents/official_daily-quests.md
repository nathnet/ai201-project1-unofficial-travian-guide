# Daily Quests

> Source: Travian: Legends Support  
> URL: https://support.travian.com/en/articles/16-daily-quests

---

Daily Quests are a great way to earn **extra rewards** while you play. Each day you’ll receive up to 10 tasks — some easy, some more challenging. Completing them not only gives you points but also unlocks valuable prizes.

---

## How Daily Quests Work

- Each quest you finish earns **points**.
- The more points you collect, the more rewards you unlock.
- You can earn rewards at **25, 50, 75, and 100 points** — up to **4 rewards per day**.
- Quests and rewards **reset once per day** at the same time your gameworld started.

	- Example: if your server started at 09:00, quests reset daily at 09:00.
- **Important:** You must collect your rewards before reset — otherwise, they are lost.

---

## Rewards You Can Earn

Rewards cycle in a fixed order (not random), and every player on the same gameworld sees the same reward set.

Here’s what you can get:

| 25 points | 50 points | 75 points | 100 points |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| +50 Hero Experience | +1 day +25% Lumber production | +5 Tablets | +20.000 Resources of one random type |
| +50 Culture Points | +1 day +25% Clay production | +5 Small Bandages | +400 Hero Experience |
| +1.000 Resources of one random type | +1 day +25% Iron production | +5 Cages | +4.000 Resources of each type |
| +200 Resources of each type | +1 day +25% Crop production | +1 additional Adventure | +400 Culture Points |
| repeat from the top | +1 day Travian Plus | +5 Ointments | repeat from the top |
|  | repeat from the top | repeat from the top |  |

## Tips for Daily Quests

- Rewards activate **immediately** when you claim them (e.g., Travian Plus, bonus production).
- Server speed does **not** affect rewards.
- Some quests require bulk actions — for example, **train 20 units at once**. Training them individually will not count.
- Even if you miss a day, rewards keep cycling in the same order.

---

**In short:** Check your Daily Quests every day, hit the 100-point mark if you can, and collect your rewards before reset. It’s one of the easiest ways to boost your progress!
