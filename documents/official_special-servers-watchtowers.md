# Special Servers - Watchtowers

> Source: Travian: Legends Support  
> URL: https://support.travian.com/en/articles/78-special-servers-watchtowers

---

**Prerequisites:** A wall of any type. Watchtowers can only be built in **cities**, and cities are available only on **Annual Special** servers.

Watchtowers are an improvement of existing walls and provide an additional defensive bonus for all defending units in the city, similar to walls. They are not separate buildings; they are an upgrade attached to your walls. Watchtowers **cannot be directly targeted** by enemy rams or catapults. However, if the walls are destroyed, the watchtowers are destroyed with them.

You can upgrade watchtowers up to **level 20**, and this is independent of the current wall level. Each watchtower level adds a **flat 1% defensive bonus** to all units defending the city. This bonus is applied separately from the wall bonus. For example, a level 10 watchtower increases the defensive strength of defending units by exactly **10%**. Watchtowers also **increase the population** of the city without adding additional crop consumption, unlike standard buildings.
