# Troop Upgrades and Smithy

> Source: Travian: Legends Support  
> URL: https://support.travian.com/en/articles/40-troop-upgrades-and-smithy

---

The strength of your troops in **attack** and **defense** can be improved in several ways — through **hero items**, **Smithy upgrades**, and **alliance bonuses**.

---

## 1. Hero Items

### Weapons

Equipping your hero with **right-hand weapons** increases the **attack and defense values** of specific troop types.
> [Hero Item Overview and Mounts](https://support.travian.com/articles/89)

### Horn of the Natarian

This special hero item increases the **attack strength** of your hero and all accompanying troops **when attacking the Natars**.

---

## 2. Alliance Metallurgy Bonus

Your alliance can unlock the **Metallurgy Bonus**, which boosts both **offensive** and **defensive power** for all members’ troops.
> [Alliance Bonuses](https://support.travian.com/articles/88)

---

## 3. The Smithy

The **Smithy** improves the weapons and armor of your units.

- Each upgrade increases the **combat efficiency** of a troop type.
- The higher the **Smithy level**, the more you can improve your units.
- The improvement level is **specific to the village** where the Smithy is located.

### Key Details

- Upgrades apply to **all units of that type trained in the same village**.
- Units trained in another village are **not affected**.
- The **effect strength** depends on the **unit’s crop consumption** — higher-upkeep units gain a larger improvement per upgrade level.
- When **troop merging** is enabled, merged units inherit the **upgrade level** of the **village they are moved to**.

---

**Tip:**
Always keep upgrading your Smithy as your army grows. Even small improvements across many troops add up to a massive difference in battle outcomes.
