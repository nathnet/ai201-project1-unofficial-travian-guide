# Hero Armour Items

> Source: Travian: Legends Support  
> URL: https://support.travian.com/en/articles/93-hero-armour-items

---

Hero armour items provide bonuses to experience gain, health regeneration, damage reduction, fighting strength, and troop training speed. Each equipment category—**Helmets**, **Armour**, and **Boots**—is divided into **Tier 1, Tier 2, and Tier 3**, with higher tiers offering stronger effects.

This guide explains what each item does so you can choose the right equipment for your hero.

---

## **Helmets**

## **Tier 1**

- **Helmet of Awareness** – +15% experience
- **Helmet of Regeneration** – +10 HP regeneration/day
- **Helmet of the Gladiator**

	- +100 CP/day on 1x servers
	- +50 CP/day on 3x servers
- **Helmet of the Horseman** – +10% cavalry training
- **Helmet of the Mercenary** – +10% infantry training

## **Tier 2**

- **Helmet of Enlightenment** – +20% experience
- **Helmet of Health** – +15 HP regeneration/day
- **Helmet of the Tribune**

	- +400 CP/day on 1x
	- +200 CP/day on 3x
- **Helmet of the Cavalry** – +15% cavalry training
- **Helmet of the Warrior** – +15% infantry training

## **Tier 3**

- **Helmet of Wisdom** – +25% experience
- **Helmet of Healing** – +20 HP regeneration/day
- **Helmet of the Consul**

	- +1600 CP/day on 1x
	- +800 CP/day on 3x
- **Helmet of the Heavy Cavalry** – +20% cavalry training
- **Helmet of the Archon** – +20% infantry training

---

## **Armour**

## **Tier 1**

- **Armour of Regeneration** – +20 HP/day
- **Light Scale Armour** – Damage −4 HP; +10 HP/day
- **Light Breastplate** – +250 fighting strength
- **Light Segmented Armour** – Damage −3; +125 fighting strength

## **Tier 2**

- **Armour of Health** – +30 HP/day
- **Scale Armour** – Damage −6 HP; +15 HP/day
- **Breastplate** – +1000 fighting strength
- **Segmented Armour** – Damage −4; +500 fighting strength

## **Tier 3**

- **Armour of Healing** – +40 HP/day
- **Heavy Scale Armour** – Damage −8 HP; +20 HP/day
- **Heavy Breastplate** – +4000 fighting strength
- **Heavy Segmented Armour** – Damage −5; +2000 fighting strength

---

## **Boots**

## **Tier 1**

- **Boots of Regeneration** – +10 HP/day
- **Boots of the Mercenary** – +25% troop speed after the first 20 fields (hero must travel with the army)
- **Small Spurs**

	- +3 fields/hour on 1x
	- +6 fields/hour on 3x

## **Tier 2**

- **Boots of Health** – +15 HP/day
- **Boots of the Warrior** – +50% troop speed after the first 20 fields
- **Spurs**

	- +4 fields/hour on 1x
	- +8 fields/hour on 3x

## **Tier 3**

- **Boots of Healing** – +20 HP/day
- **Boots of the Archon** – +75% troop speed after the first 20 fields
- **Great Spurs**

	- +5 fields/hour on 1x
	- +10 fields/hour on 3x
