# Interacting with Other Players

> Source: Travian: Legends Support  
> URL: https://support.travian.com/en/articles/11-interacting-with-other-players

---

Travian: Legends is a multiplayer game at its core. Your success depends not only on your own strategy but also on how you interact with others. Here are the main ways you can cooperate — or compete — with fellow players.

---

## 1. Attacking Other Players️

- You can attack anyone who is **not** in Beginner’s Protection, on vacation, or banned.
- Choose a target via:

	- the **map**
	- the **statistics**
	- or by entering village details directly in the **Rally Point**

**Attack types:**

- **Normal attack:** Fought until one side is completely destroyed.
- **Raid:** Smaller engagement — catapults won’t fire, chiefs won’t lower loyalty, rams won’t damage walls. The attacker usually keeps at least 1 surviving soldier.

---

## 2. Reinforcing Allies️

Help your alliance or confederacy by sending reinforcements.

- Choose a target the same way as with attacks.
- Reinforcements stay until they die, you withdraw them, or the village owner sends them back.
- **Important:** The **village owner pays upkeep** for all reinforcement troops.
- If your alliance or confederacy connection ends, reinforcements are automatically returned (with some delay).

	- [Learn about Alliance, Confed & NAP](https://support.travian.com/articles/84)

---

## 3. Trading Resources

The **Marketplace** allows you to send and exchange resources.

- Options:

	- Accept an existing offer
	- Create your own offer
	- Send resources directly to another village
- During **Beginner’s Protection**, you can only send resources manually to players in your own alliance/confederacy.
- You don’t need a marketplace to **receive** resources, but without one you can’t see incoming shipments.
- Marketplace trades can also happen with players outside your alliance or confederacy.

---

## 4. Messaging Other Players

Use messages to communicate, coordinate, or negotiate.

- Open the **message symbol** to read, write, or manage messages.
- To send: enter the player’s nickname, add a subject and content, then click **send**.
- Alliance leaders may allow special roles to send group messages:

	- `[ally]` → sends to the whole alliance
	- `[def]` → sends to defense coordinators
	- `[off]` → sends to offense coordinators
- Gold Club feature: archive your reports and messages for easier management.

	- [About Gold Club](https://support.travian.com/articles/128)

---

## Dealing with Spam

If you receive spam, simply click **Report as spam** (next to the delete button) to forward it directly to the Player Safety Team.

---

By mastering these four methods — attacking, reinforcing, trading, and messaging — you’ll be able to build strong alliances, defend effectively, and rise to power in Travian: Legends.
