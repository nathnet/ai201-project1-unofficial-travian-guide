# The Teutons and Their Advantages

> Source: Travian: Legends Support  
> URL: https://support.travian.com/en/articles/8-the-teutons-and-their-advantages

---

### Overview

The **Teutons** are the most aggressive and fearless of all the tribes. Their warriors are infamous for their **rage in battle** and relentless **pillage raids**. Unafraid of death, they roam the lands in overwhelming numbers, striking fear into their enemies.

While Teutonic troops lack the discipline and organization of the **Romans** and **Gauls**, they make up for it with sheer **ferocity and numbers**. Their units are **cheap to train**, **fast to produce**, and have **excellent carrying capacity**, making them ideal for **early-game raiding** and **constant aggression**.

> The Teutons are a great choice for **offense-minded and experienced players** who enjoy fast-paced, high-pressure gameplay focused on raiding and domination.

---

### Playstyle Tips

- Focus on **raiding early and often** — your troops are cheap, expendable, and perfect for resource theft.
- Use your **Brewery** to increase your attack power when planning major assaults.
- Your **Earth Wall** is hard to destroy but offers limited defense — rely on offense, not fortifications.
- Keep your hero active in raids to benefit from the **Cranny dip bonus**.
- Be prepared to expand aggressively — Teutons thrive when they maintain constant pressure on enemies.

---

### Special Features

| **Earth Wall** | Nearly **indestructible**, but provides only a **small defensive bonus**. |
| --- | --- |
| **Merchants** | Can carry **1,000 resources** at a speed of **12 fields/hour**. |
| **Troops** | **Cheap, fast to train**, and have **the best carrying capacity** in the game. |
| **Chief** | The Teutonic administrator; lowers enemy village **loyalty by 20–25%**. |
| **Special Building – Brewery** | Increases **attacking strength** of Teutonic troops. *(Note: cannot build Residence, Palace, or Command Center in the same village.)* [Brewery](https://support.travian.com/en/support/solutions/articles/7000065345) |
| **Hero Bonus** | Gains **+20% Cranny dip (Plunder bonus)** for all troops attacking with the hero, allowing you to **steal more resources** from enemies. [Hiding Resources & Cranny](https://support.travian.com/en/support/solutions/articles/7000068298) |

---

### Strategy Summary

**Strengths:** Cheap and fast troops, best raiders in the game, strong early aggression.
**Weaknesses:** Weak defense and slower units; struggles in prolonged defensive wars.

> If you enjoy **relentless offense, early domination, and overwhelming your enemies through numbers**, the Teutons are the tribe for you.
