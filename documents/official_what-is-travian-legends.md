# What is Travian: Legends?

> Source: Travian: Legends Support  
> URL: https://support.travian.com/en/articles/138-what-is-travian-legends

---

*Travian: Legends* is a free browser-based **Build & Raid strategy game**. You begin with a small village in an ancient European world and grow it into a thriving empire through clever **economic, military, and diplomatic** decisions. Every choice you make affects not just your own progress, but also the world around you — and other players.

The ultimate goal?
Build a **Wonder of the World** to level 100 and lead your alliance to victory.
Learn more here: [Wonder of the World](https://support.travian.com/articles/103)

---

### Your Choices Matter

Your very first decision already shapes your playstyle — choosing your **tribe**.
In *Travian: Legends*, you can play as one of six unique tribes, each with their own strengths and strategy preferences:

- **Huns** or **Teutons** – Best for aggressive players who enjoy raiding and early combat.
- **Egyptians** or **Gauls** – Strong defensive tribes with good economic potential.
- **Romans** – Balanced all-rounders, ideal for long-term planners who shine in the mid and endgame.
- **Spartans** – Known for units with an excellent balance of power and crop consumption, crucial for later stages.

Learn more about each tribe: [All Tribes Overview](https://support.travian.com/articles/3)

---

### The Game is Played in Rounds

Each game world runs for a limited time — called a **round**.
When it ends, winners are announced and the world resets. After that, players re-register for a new round and start fresh.

This cycle keeps the game exciting, giving you the chance to:

- Try new **tribes**
- Experiment with different **strategies**
- Play on different **server speeds** (e.g., faster rounds for more active play)

---

### First Steps

Once you register, you start as the leader of a small village surrounded by others.
Your first goal is to **build a foundation** for your empire.

- Use your **beginner’s protection** to grow safely:
During this period, no one can attack you or steal your resources.
Learn more: [Beginner’s Protection](https://support.travian.com/articles/12)
- Focus on **economic development** first — upgrading resource fields and preparing for your second village.
- Avoid training troops too early, as troop upkeep can slow your progress.
Once your economy is stable, start developing your army and defense.

Balancing your **economy** and **military** is key to long-term success.

---

### Choosing Your Strategy

When your beginner’s protection ends, you’ll face your first major decision:
How will you protect your resources and expand?

You could:

- **Build crannies** to hide resources from attackers.
- **Train defensive troops** to make raids on you unprofitable.
- **Create an offensive army** to raid others instead.
- **Join an alliance** for protection and cooperation.

There is no single best way — it depends on your preferred playstyle and your surroundings.

---

### Alliances and Cooperation

*Travian: Legends* is as much about people as it is about strategy.
Soon, nearby players will notice your growth. Some may become allies, others rivals.

Joining an **alliance** gives you vital support, especially for defense and coordinated attacks.
To join or create one, you need an **Embassy** building.

Stronger alliances often recruit active, well-developed players. If you prove your reliability, you might get invitations from top groups — or start one yourself. Learn how to manage alliances here: [Creating, Joining, and Leaving an Alliance](https://support.travian.com/articles/85)

---

### The Power of Teamwork

Alliances add a deeper layer of gameplay and are essential for reaching victory.
Only well-organized alliances can:

- Capture and use special **Artefacts** that grant bonuses → [Artefacts Guide](https://support.travian.com/articles/101)
- Build the **World Wonder**, a massive structure that decides the winner of the round → [Building a World Wonder](https://support.travian.com/en/articles/153)

Success in *Travian: Legends* requires teamwork, coordination, and trust — often forming real friendships between players. Many return round after round for this reason alone.

If you want to experience even more, explore our **special events and tournaments**: [Special Gameworlds](https://support.travian.com/articles/20)

---

### Summary

In *Travian: Legends*:

- You start small and grow through smart strategy.
- Choose a tribe that fits your playstyle.
- Build, defend, and expand your empire.
- Join forces with others to conquer Artefacts and build the World Wonder.
- Compete, cooperate, and make your mark before the world resets.

Every decision matters — and every round offers a new chance to become a legend.
