# Central Village Overview

> Source: Travian: Legends Support  
> URL: https://support.travian.com/en/articles/42-central-village-overview

---

The **Central Village Overview** is a **Travian Plus** premium feature that gives you a quick, central view of all your villages and their key stats.

You can open it by clicking on your **village names** above the village list on the right-hand side of the game screen.

> [Travian Plus Membership](https://support.travian.com/articles/127)

---

## Overview Tab

Shows all troop and building activity per village.

- **Attack icons:** Indicate active or incoming troop movements. Hover over them to see the number of movements.
- **Building column:** Displays how many constructions are currently in progress or queued.
- **Troops column:** Shows which unit types are being trained.

---

## Resources Tab

Displays total and per-village **resource stock** for wood, clay, iron, and crop.

- Also shows **merchant availability** — the first number is free merchants, the second is total merchants.

---

## Warehouse Tab

Shows **warehouse and granary capacity** for each village.

- Displays **time until full or empty** for each resource type.
- If **crop is negative**, it appears in **red** — when the timer reaches zero, troops begin to **starve** unless the granary is refilled.

---

## Culture Points Tab

Summarizes your village’s **Culture Point (CP)** progress:

- Daily CP production rate.
- Active celebrations and their durations.
- Number of **settlers** and **administrators**.
- Used vs. available **expansion slots**.

---

## Troops Tab

Provides detailed troop information for every village:

- **All troops overview:** Total count of each troop type across all villages.
- **Troops in village subtab:** Shows units stationed in each village and their **crop upkeep**.
- **Smithy subtab:** Displays **research levels** for all unit types.
- **Hospital subtab:** Shows **wounded troops** and their **healing progress**.

---

**Tip:**
The Central Village Overview is a powerful management tool — use it to coordinate defenses, balance resources, and spot issues (like starving troops) before they become critical.
