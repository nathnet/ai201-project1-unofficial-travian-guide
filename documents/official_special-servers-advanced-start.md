# Special Servers - Advanced Start

> Source: Travian: Legends Support  
> URL: https://support.travian.com/en/articles/28-special-servers-advanced-start

---

Some special gameworlds include the **Advanced Start** feature.
If it’s active, it will always be **announced before the gameworld begins**.

---

## Features

- Your **first village** starts with **all resource fields at level 5**.
- You already have **six settlers** and enough **Culture Points (CP)** to settle **two additional villages** immediately.
- **18-croppers**, **15-croppers**, and **9-croppers** **cannot** be chosen as your **second or third villages**.
- You receive enough Culture Points to settle two villages **plus 75% of the CP required for the fourth** (your progress bar will be filled to three-quarters).
- The **first two settled villages** automatically start with **resource fields at level 5**.
- Any **further villages** are settled **normally** with level 0 fields.

---

**Tip:** The Advanced Start helps you expand much faster — plan your first two villages carefully to make the most of this early boost.
