# BB-codes

> Source: Travian: Legends Support  
> URL: https://support.travian.com/en/articles/160-bb-codes

---

BB-codes let you format text and insert smart links in alliance pages and in-game messages (e.g., link to coords, players, alliances). Many codes have buttons in the editor; the list below covers the ones you’ll likely type by hand.

## Send to groups (message “Recipient” field)

- `[ally]` — send to the whole alliance (requires permission).
- `[off]` — send to members marked with the **offensive** specialization.
- `[def]` — send to members marked with the **defensive** specialization.

---

## Text color

Wrap any text:
`[colour=red]Warning at (100|-10)[/colour]`
Available colors: `white, red, yellow, green, blue, purple`.

---

## Clickable links

`[url=http://example.com]Link name[/url]` — creates a clickable link.

---

## Annual Special (regions)

`[region]ID or Name[/region]` — links to the region page (Annual Special only).

---

## Alliance internal page stats

- `[stats]strength[/stats]` — shows your alliance strength vs. previous data.
- `[stats]fighting points[/stats]` — shows attack + defense points gained today.

---

## News feed (alliance activity)

`[news]10[/news]` — shows recent invitations, joins/leaves, conquests, foundings, losses; number is how many items per page (1–50).

---

## War tracking

`[losses]AllianceID[/losses]` — summary of your results vs. a specific alliance (troops destroyed, resources stolen).

---

### Tips

- Test codes in a draft message or hidden alliance section before announcing widely.
- Keep colors for emphasis only; overuse reduces readability.
