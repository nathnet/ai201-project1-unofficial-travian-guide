# How we enforce the game rules

> Source: Travian: Legends Support  
> URL: https://support.travian.com/en/articles/120-how-we-enforce-the-game-rules

---

**How We Enforce the Game Rules**

To keep *Travian: Legends* fair and enjoyable for everyone, Travian Games monitors gameplay and enforces the [Game Rules](https://www.travian.com/international/gamerules) and our [Terms & Conditions](https://agb.traviangames.com/).

---

**1. Rule Enforcement Overview**

We take action against accounts that violate the Game Rules or Terms & Conditions. Depending on the situation, this can include temporary or permanent restrictions, adjustments to game progress, the removal of unfair advantages, and, in severe cases, account or avatar deletion.

Our goal is always the same: **to protect fair play and maintain balance** in every game world.

---

**2. Detection and Oversight**

We use automated systems and manual checks to identify potential rule violations or suspicious activity that could affect fair play.

Most detected cases are **manually reviewed by our team** before any action is taken.

In some situations, certain actions may be applied automatically to quickly restore balance in the game world.

All detection and review processes are regularly evaluated to ensure that enforcement remains **accurate, proportionate, and fair**.

---

**3. Transparency and Player Information**

When an account is penalized, the affected player will receive a notification explaining the reason for the action.

To protect the integrity of our detection systems, we do not disclose detailed technical information about how these systems work.

---

**4. Appeals**

If you believe a decision was incorrect, you can request a review by contacting **Customer Support** via ticket.

Each appeal is **reviewed by a member of our team** to ensure that every case receives human attention and oversight.

---

**5. Our Commitment**

We apply rules consistently across all game worlds, treat every report and appeal with care, and work to ensure a fair and enjoyable experience for all players.
