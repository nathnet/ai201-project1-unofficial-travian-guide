# 3 Useful Things

> Source: Travian: Legends Support  
> URL: https://support.travian.com/en/articles/159-3-useful-things

---

## 1. Quick daily quest credit with scouts

To complete the daily quests “Raid an unoccupied oasis” and “Raid/attack a Natarian village,” you can send **just 1 scout** to any unoccupied oasis and to any independent Natar village. If you use Farmlist, add nearby oases/Natars to finish these quickly.

## 2. Paste coordinates into a single field

You don’t need both X and Y. Paste the full `(x|y)` into **either** the X **or** Y input—the game fills the other automatically. This speeds up navigation and reduces typos.

## 3. Auto-insert coordinates into messages, Marketplace, and Rally Point

With [Travian Plus](https://support.travian.com/articles/126), clicking a village’s coordinates in your village list auto-fills those coordinates in the **Marketplace** and **Rally Point**. The same click also inserts the coordinates (with the correct BB-codes) directly into **in-game messages**, so you can share targets fast.
