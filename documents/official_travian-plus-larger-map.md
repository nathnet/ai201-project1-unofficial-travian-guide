# Travian Plus: Larger Map

> Source: Travian: Legends Support  
> URL: https://support.travian.com/en/articles/130-travian-plus-larger-map

---

The **large fullscreen map** is a [**Travian Plus**](https://support.travian.com/articles/126) feature that gives you a wider view of your surroundings in *Travian: Legends*.

---

### How to Open the Larger Map

1. Click the **expand icon** on your regular map.
2. The page will refresh, and the fullscreen version of the map will load.
3. To return to the normal view, click the **normal icon** in the top-left corner.

The large map uses the **entire browser window**, helping you plan troop movements, manage villages, and navigate the world more comfortably.
