# Travian Tournament Overview

> Source: Travian: Legends Support  
> URL: https://support.travian.com/en/articles/107-travian-tournament-overview

---

Every year, the best Travian: Legends players gather to compete in the **Travian Tournament** — a global competition for **eternal glory and exclusive prizes**. The tournament is divided into **two stages**: **Qualifications** and **Finals**.

---

### 1. Qualification Stage

The **Qualification rounds** begin every year in **June** and take place across **four regional game worlds**:

- **America**
- **Arabics**
- **Asia**
- **Europe**

Keep an eye on the **Travian: Legends news section** and the **game world calendar** on [travian.com](https://www.travian.com) for the official start date and registration details.

#### **Who Can Join**

Everyone can register and participate — but only the strongest will advance.

To **earn a key for the Finals**, you must:

- Remain active until the end of the Qualification server, and
- Have **at least 10 villages** by the end of the round.

Learn more about rewards here: [Travian Tournament Prizes](https://support.travian.com/articles/109)

---

### 2. Finals Stage

The **Finals** bring together the best players from all Qualification regions.
This stage takes place on **one global server** in the **International region**, starting in **November**.
Exact dates are published in the **news** and **game world calendar** once announced.

#### How to Join the Finals

Registration for the Finals is **by key only**:

- Most keys are awarded to successful players from the Qualification rounds.
- A **wild card raffle** also gives players a second chance to join — this takes place a few weeks before the Finals start.

If you missed the Qualification round, be sure to **register for the raffle** to get your shot at competing in the Finals!

---

### 3. Hall of Fame

Beyond prizes and trophies, the **Travian Tournament Hall of Fame** immortalizes player and alliance achievements for future generations.

The Hall of Fame records:

- Player and alliance rankings
- Offensive and defensive performance
- Population statistics
- World Wonder progress
- Hero achievements
- Player names, ranks, points, and countries

Explore past champions here: [Travian Tournament Hall of Fame](https://tournament.travian.com/hall-of-fame)
