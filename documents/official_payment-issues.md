# Payment Issues

> Source: Travian: Legends Support  
> URL: https://support.travian.com/en/articles/114-payment-issues

---

### Why Was My Payment Rejected or Declined?

There are several possible reasons why your payment might not go through.
Check the following common causes before trying again:

- **Previous chargeback:**
If your account or credit card had a chargeback in the past, that payment method can no longer be used.
- **Incorrect details entered:**
You have **three attempts** to enter the correct payment information. After that, the system will block additional tries temporarily.
- **Transaction limits:**
There is a **limit on the number of payments** that can be made within 48 hours for both credit cards and direct debit.
- **Insufficient balance:**
Make sure your card or account has enough funds to complete the purchase.
- **International payments blocked:**
Some banks require explicit authorization for international transactions. Contact your credit card company to enable them.
- **Unsupported card types:**
Certain cards (like **VISA Electron** or **VISA Gift Cards**) are not accepted. Try one of the other payment options listed in the store.

---

### Popups Must Be Enabled

The payment window opens as a **popup**. If it doesn’t appear, your browser may be blocking it.
See our article: [Cannot Start Purchase](https://support.travian.com/articles/113)

Make sure popups are allowed **before starting the payment**.

---

### Special Promotions

When a **special promotion** appears, be prepared to pay immediately:

- Ensure your **payment method is ready** and **popups are enabled**.
- Once clicked, some promotions **disappear and can’t be selected again**.
