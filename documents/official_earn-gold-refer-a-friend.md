# Earn Gold - Refer a Friend

> Source: Travian: Legends Support  
> URL: https://support.travian.com/en/articles/116-earn-gold-refer-a-friend

---

You can earn **free Gold** in Travian: Legends by inviting your friends to join the game! When your invited friends settle or conquer new villages, you’ll automatically receive rewards

### How It Works

![](../assets/297e26aab6_20260427082953_3370.gif)Invite your friends using your **referral link**.

- Your friend must register for the **same gameworld** using that link.
- You’ll receive Gold when your friend settles or conquers new villages:

	- Villages **2 to 9:** 20 Gold each
	- Village **10:** 40 Gold
	→ That’s **up to 200 Gold per friend**!

You can earn rewards from up to **10 invited friends** — meaning a total of **2,000 Gold** if all reach their 10th village.

> Even if your referred friend loses a village later, you keep the Gold already earned. However, you won’t get another reward for a replacement village.

### Inviting Players

You can invite **as many players** as you want, but only **10** can count toward your Gold rewards at the same time. If one of them stops progressing, a new invited player can take their place to help you reach the maximum reward
