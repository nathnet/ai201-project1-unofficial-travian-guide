# Maximize Your Hero’s XP

> Source: Travian: Legends Support  
> URL: https://support.travian.com/en/articles/169-maximize-your-hero-s-xp

---

## 1. How battle XP works

- **XP = enemy crop consumption destroyed.** Each supply point of a killed unit grants **1 XP** to the attacker’s hero.
- **Defense splits by crop, not just strength.** When multiple defenders send heroes, the XP share goes to the defending heroes **proportional to the crop consumption** of the army accompanying each hero (not purely by hero strength).
- Example outcome: equal defensive values can still give **more XP** to the defender whose army eats more crop.

## 2. Other XP sources (quick wins)

- **Adventures & Daily Quests**
- **Village tasks**
- **Scrolls** (+10 XP on use)
- **Helmets** (e.g., **Helmet of Awareness** adds % XP gain; it multiplies whatever XP you’d get).

## 3. Oasis farming = steady XP + resources

Sending your hero to **unoccupied oases** is one of the best XP grinds:

- Your hero gets **1 XP per Nature unit’s crop consumption** and **resources** per kill (e.g., Rat/Spider = **40 of each**, Elephant = **200 of each**).
- You can send the **hero alone or with troops**; **all XP goes to the hero** from this source.
- Recommended kit for speed & safety:

	- **Horse + Small Spurs** (faster trips)
	- **Small Map** (faster returns)
	- **Right-hand weapon** (combat strength)
	- **Helmet of Awareness** (more XP)
	- **Light Scale Armor** + **Ointments** (survivability and heal after the fight)
	- **Bandages** if using expensive cav escorts
	- Optional **1 Cage** as insurance if an oasis rolls a tough spawn (prevents battle and nets a pet).

## 4. Attribute points: what to level and when

- **Early game default:** put most points into **Resource Production**. Add a **few Strength** so adventures don’t chunk your HP.
- **Strength** becomes more valuable if you’re actively **fighting or oasis-farming**.
- **Off bonus:** +**0.2%** army attack per point (max **+20%**). Applies only when your hero **attacks with** the army.
- **Def bonus:** +**0.2%** army defense per point (max **+20%**). Applies only when your hero **defends with** your own troops. (Reinforcements from others don’t get your hero’s bonus.)
- Rule of thumb: for **large armies**, Off/Def bonus can outscale raw Strength; for **small skirmishes**, Strength is often better. A calculator can help check your breakpoint.

## 5. Low-risk leveling tricks

- **Level-up heal:** If health is low, finish **tasks/quests** to pop a level—your hero returns to **full HP**.
- **Heal timing:** Use **Ointments after** the battle, not before.
- **Risk control:** If an oasis suddenly spikes in strength, **equip a cage mid-travel** to avoid the fight.

---

### Quick checklist

- Farm **unoccupied oases** regularly for XP + resources.
- Wear **Helmet of Awareness** while grinding.
- Early game: **Resource Production**, then pivot to **Strength**/**Off/Def bonus** based on your role.
- In defense with multiple heroes, remember: **XP splits by crop** of each accompanying army.
