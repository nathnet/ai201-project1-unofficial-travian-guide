# How to Earn Gold

> Source: Travian: Legends Support  
> URL: https://support.travian.com/en/articles/161-how-to-earn-gold

---

You can get gold without (or before) spending money. Use the options below to build a steady gold supply.

## 1. Refer a Friend

Invite new players and earn gold as they grow:

- Villages 2–9 of your invitee: **20 gold each** (8×20 = 160).
- Village 10 of your invitee: **40 gold**.
- You can receive each milestone reward **up to 10 times** (for 10 different invitees).
- **Total possible:** **2,000 gold**.
If a friend later loses a village, you **keep** the reward already earned.

## 2. Use the Auction (Silver → Gold)

- Exchange rate: **200 silver = 1 gold** (shown in the Exchange Office UI on page 3).
- Early game, sell **consumables** you don’t need (especially **cages** and **ointments**) to generate starter silver.
- Found a valuable item early? Consider selling it now—prices often fall once **Tier 2/3** items appear, and you can usually buy back cheaper later.
- Smart, timely gold use typically beats holding a single strong hero item.

## 3. One-Time Offer (Low-cost Starter Pack)

- Appears after a few hours up to a day of active play (depends on server speed).
- Great value for early momentum.
- **Non-transferable**: gold from this offer can only be used on the **gameworld where it was bought**.
- Availability window: **24 hours**.
