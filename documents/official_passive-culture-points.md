# Passive culture points

> Source: Travian: Legends Support  
> URL: https://support.travian.com/en/articles/174-passive-culture-points

---

**Culture Points (CP)** determine how many villages you can **settle or conquer** in *Travian: Legends*.
You gain them by constructing and upgrading buildings. You can view your total CP and daily production in your **Administration Building**. Generating enough CP is key to expanding your empire — more villages mean more production, more troops, and greater strategic power.

---

### How to Gain Culture Points

There are three main ways to earn culture points:

1. **Using** [Artworks](https://support.travian.com/articles/90) **-**Consumed via your hero’s inventory.
2. **Holding** [Celebrations](https://support.travian.com/articles/82) **-**Small and Great Celebrations in the Townhall provide CP boosts.
3. **Increasing Passive Culture Point Production**
This method gives steady, automatic CP each day — no action needed after setup.

---

### What Is Passive Culture Points?

**Passive CP** refers to culture points generated daily by your buildings and resource fields.
It is the foundation of your account’s long-term expansion and should be developed in all **resource villages**, not just your capital. Even if you’re not focusing on celebrations or artifacts, every resource village can contribute valuable CP through its building layout.

### Practical Tips for Efficient CP Growth

- **Remove buildings when no longer needed:**
After researching units in the Smithy or Academy, you can demolish them to free space for Warehouses or Granaries without losing progress.
- **Walls provide hidden value:**
Every village with a wall gains not only defense but also better scout protection.
- **Keep scouts in every village:**
100 scouts per village is the minimum for warnings; key villages should have 500–1000.
- **Choose good oasis locations:**
Oases nearby increase your overall production, indirectly supporting faster CP generation.

---

### Summary

Passive culture point generation is a low-effort, high-reward investment that steadily expands your empire’s growth potential.

To optimize:

- Include high-CP buildings in every village.
- Maintain balanced development — not all CP must come from your capital.
- Replace unneeded structures with Warehouses, Granaries, or other utility buildings when upgrades are complete.

A well-planned passive CP setup ensures you can always settle or conquer new villages when the opportunity arises.
