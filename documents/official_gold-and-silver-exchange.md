# Gold and Silver Exchange

> Source: Travian: Legends Support  
> URL: https://support.travian.com/en/articles/115-gold-and-silver-exchange

---

You can exchange **Gold and Silver** directly in the **Auction House** through the Exchange Office feature.

- **1 Gold = 100 Silver**
- **200 Silver = 1 Gold**

This is a **premium feature** available to all players inside the Auction House
