# Northern Legends - Game Mechanics

> Source: Travian: Legends Support  
> URL: https://support.travian.com/en/articles/32-northern-legends-game-mechanics

---

**Northern Legends** is the **2024 Annual Special** of Travian: Legends. Below is an overview of the gameplay features that differ from regular gameworlds.

---

## **Main Features Kept from Previous Annual Specials**

- Uses the **Ancient Europe map** with region-based spawning. The map is **flat** and **edges cannot be crossed**.

	- [Special Servers - Regional Map](https://support.travian.com/articles/27)
- Includes **87 conquerable regions** that generate **Victory Points** instead of a World Wonder endgame.

	- [Special Servers - Regions and Population](https://support.travian.com/articles/26)
- Victory Points are **stored in villages**.

	- [Special Servers - Ancient Powers and Victory Points](https://support.travian.com/articles/106)
- **Keep Tribe on Conquest** is enabled.
- **Troop forwarding** (including bulk forwarding) and **troop merging** remain available.

	- [Special Servers - Forwarding Troops](https://support.travian.com/articles/72)
	- [Special Servers - Merging Troops](https://support.travian.com/articles/73)
- **Cities** and **Watchtowers** are included.

	- [Special Servers - Cities](https://support.travian.com/articles/79)
	- [Special Servers - Watchtowers](https://support.travian.com/articles/78)
- **Alliance attack and raid visibility** is available in the alliance members list.

	- [Special Servers - Alliance Attack Notifications](https://support.travian.com/articles/31)
- **Naval warfare and trading** are available. Players can settle on the shore to build ships for moving troops and resources quickly across the sea.

	- [Special Servers - Harbor and Deep Water](https://support.travian.com/articles/24)
	- [Special Servers - Ships and Naval Movement](https://support.travian.com/articles/25)
- **No confederacies**.
- Resource deliveries by merchants or trade ships can be **cancelled within 60 seconds**.

---

## **New Features**

### **Vikings as a New Tribe**

Teutons are not present on Northern Legends. Their place is taken by **Vikings**, a new tribe specializing in sea-based warfare and raiding.

Key Viking traits:

- **Berserker**: A strong infantry unit that consumes twice the crop but deals **additional damage upon death** when attacking.
- **Valkyrie’s Blessing** cavalry: Very efficient in crop consumption.
- Viking heroes can **reduce enemy village loyalty by 5%**, making them powerful offensive leaders.

### **Raid Ships**

A new ship type optimized for **raiding via farmlists**.
Vikings have the **fastest raid ships**, giving them an advantage at sea.

---

## **Changed Pathfinding**

Ship movement now always follows a **straight-line path**, whether traveling over land or sea. The older, more complex pathfinding system has been removed.

---

## **Faster Construction**

Construction time for the **Main Building, Granary, Warehouse** (including great variants) and all **resource fields** is reduced by **25%**, enabling faster economic development.

---

## **Invincible Rome (Northern Legends Only)**

To compensate for the absence of Teuton spearmen, Roman **Legionnaires** receive significant boosts:

- Cavalry defense increased from **50 → 70**
- Training time reduced from **26:40 → 22:40**
- Movement speed increased from **6 → 7 fields/hour**

These buffs apply **exclusively** to Northern Legends.

---

## **Improved Alliance Bonuses**

- The **commerce bonus** now increases merchant speed as well as capacity.
- The **philosophy bonus** improves Culture Point production and affects **Town Hall celebrations and artworks**, raising their limits.

	- [Alliance Bonuses](https://support.travian.com/articles/88)
	- [Celebrations and Town Hall](https://support.travian.com/articles/82)

---

## **Choose the Tribe of the First Three Settled Villages**

Players may choose the tribe for their **first three settled villages**.
This applies only to settled villages—**conquered villages do not count**.
If these villages are lost, the option **cannot** be used again.
