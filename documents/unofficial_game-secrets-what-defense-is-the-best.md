**Welcome to the Game Secrets series!**

Even though we called our blog post like that, we have to admit: in Travian: Legends, the question “What is the best defense?” doesn’t have a universal answer. It highly depends on own player development, current game situation and even how far the player is from others. Let’s just give few examples of players possible decisions:

- Have high production, gold and live quite far on the map? Then pick the**fastest cavalry units** to be in time everywhere.
- Limited in gold and resources but still want to support alliance with defence? Then cheapest infantry (**highest defence/cost value**) would be ideal.
- The gameworld is about to become a hot place with multiple battles? Then probably we can ignore crop consumption and focus on the units that gain the defence points fastest (**highest defence/time parameter**).
- Preparing for the world wonder or just do not have huge crop production? Then **crop efficiency** is one of the keys.
- Don’t want to make lots of def villages and prefer to train infantry and cavalry in one? Then it’s better to pick the tribe that provides such an option.
