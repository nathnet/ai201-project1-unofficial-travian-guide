# Ban inquiry escalation

> Source: Travian: Legends Support  
> URL: https://support.travian.com/en/articles/123-ban-inquiry-escalation

---

A ban can be a stressful and frustrating event. We think this article can help you to understand the process behind each ban and let you feel less confused.

Check: [What should I do if my avatar is banned?](https://support.travian.com/articles/119)

So, what happens when an avatar ban occurs?

- Your avatar is banned - if you are sure you didn't break the rules, then you can **write a ticket to Customer Service** asking for details of the ban.
- After the Customer Service explanation, if you still think you are innocent, you can **ask to send the ticket to the Player Safety Team** for a ban review.
- At this point, **the Player Safety Team takes care of this case**.

Now, 2 things may happen:

- **The Player Safety Team confirms the ban is correct**

If the SRES agrees that the ban and the punishment are both correct, then they send the ticket back to Customer Service confirming the ban and the punishment.

Customer Service then respond by telling you that the Player Safety Team confirmed both ban and punishment and sends you some details shared by the Player Safety Team.

However, Customer Service is not able to share much information as they can't reveal violation detection mechanisms.

- **The Player Safety Team decides the ban is incorrect**

If the Player Safety Team decides the ban or the punishment is incorrect, then they send the ticket to the **Team Lead** for the final review.

- If the Team Lead agrees that the ban is correct, then they send the ticket back to Customer Service confirming both ban and punishment.
- If the Team Lead decides the ban reason or the punishment is incorrect, then they change the ban reason or offers the correct penalty and sends the ticket back to Customer Service explaining what changes were made.
- If the Team Lead decides that the ban was incorrect, they ban cancellation, gives the player some form of compensation for the lost game time, and sends the ticket back to Customer Service explaining what changes were made.

Ban cancellation is an offer to unban the avatar without any penalty. It can be accepted by the player at any time to ensure that avatar is not unbanned without player's knowledge.
