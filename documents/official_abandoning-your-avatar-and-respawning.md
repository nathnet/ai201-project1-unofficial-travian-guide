# Abandoning Your Avatar and Respawning

> Source: Travian: Legends Support  
> URL: https://support.travian.com/en/articles/13-abandoning-your-avatar-and-respawning

---

Sometimes your starting position in Travian: Legends isn’t ideal. Maybe your spawn location is poor, or you chose the wrong tribe or map quarter. Don’t worry — you can **abandon your avatar** and **respawn** with a fresh start.

---

## When Can You Respawn?

You can only respawn if **all** of the following are true:

- The gameworld is still open for registration
- Your avatar is not scheduled for deletion
- Your avatar is not banned
- You are still under **Beginner’s Protection**
- The Auction House is not fully unlocked (your hero hasn’t completed enough adventures yet)
- You only have **one village**
- You are the account **owner** (sitters and duals cannot use respawn)

---

## How to Respawn

1. Click the **cogwheel icon** (top corner).
2. Open the **Avatar tab**.
3. Find the **“Respawn Avatar”** option.
4. Type **respawn** in the confirmation field.
5. Choose your **map quarter**, **tribe**, and **avatar name**.

	- You can reuse the same avatar name if you like.

After confirming, your old avatar is abandoned and you receive a brand-new village in a fresh spot. Progress is not transferred — you restart from scratch.

---

## What Happens to Gold?

- **Gold Starter Promo**- Gold from this special one-time offer package is not transferable (see [Game Rule 1.8](https://www.travian.com/gamerules))
- **Purchased Gold:** Unspent Gold is automatically transferred to your new avatar, including converted time from Travian Plus and resource bonuses (if bought with transferable Gold).
- **Gold Club:** If purchased with transferable Gold, it also transfers to your new avatar.

	- [Gold Transfer details](https://support.travian.com/articles/117)

---

## What Happens to the Old Avatar?

- If your avatar was created **less than 1 hour ago**, it is deleted immediately.
- If older, it is removed from your **lobby account** (you can’t access it anymore) but remains in the game world until it’s deleted automatically later.
- If you use the same avatar name, the old one’s name is automatically changed to avoid duplicates.

---

**Tip:** Respawning is a great way to restart quickly if you feel stuck — especially in your first game. Use it early while you’re still under protection!
