# Balancing Military and Economy

> Source: Travian: Legends Support  
> URL: https://support.travian.com/en/articles/178-balancing-military-and-economy

---

Building a strong defense requires more than just troops — it’s about maintaining the right balance between **economic growth** and **military production**. This guide focuses on efficient development for **regular defensive accounts**, not World Wonder or special “fast defense” setups.

---

### Why Balancing Matters

The most effective defensive players are those who manage their **economy and military** in harmony.
Many players either:

- Accumulate resources with no plan to spend them efficiently, or
- Start too many projects and run out of resources to complete them.

Your goal is to grow at a steady pace — always having enough resources to **keep production running** without falling behind in development.

---

### Average Village Costs

To understand the balance between economy and military, it’s important to know how much each type of village costs to build and maintain:

| Village Type | Approx. Total Cost (resources) | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Resource Village** | 4 – 4.5 million | Full economic build + passive culture points |
| **Defense Village** | 7.5 – 8 million | Includes Barracks, Stables, Smithy, Tournament Square, Hospital |
| **Offensive Village** | 10+ million | Cost varies based on setup; covered separately |

*(Values based on 1x speed worlds)*

### Keeping Production Queues Running

To keep training **nonstop defense troops**, you need a steady supply of resources.

#### Basic assumptions:

- Calculations below are for **base numbers** (no helmets, bonuses, or artifacts).
- With bonuses or speed worlds, adjust accordingly.

A well-developed **resource village** produces about **8,400 resources/hour** (with bonuses and oasis adjustments).
Keep **40%** of this for ongoing development — leaving about **5,000 resources/hour** for troop training.

**> 1 defense village** requires the production of **3 support villages** to keep barracks and stables running 24/7.

**Formula:**`1 Defense Village = 3 Fully Upgraded Resource Villages`

---

### The Role of the Capital

For roughly the **first quarter of the game**, your capital should focus entirely on its own development:

- Upgrade crop fields and key infrastructure.
- Later, use it to **feed troops** or serve as an additional defense base.
- A well-developed capital can also supply other villages for fast recovery after battles.

---

### Practical Tips & Strategy

- **Use a 4-village cluster:** 1 defense village + 3 supporting ones. This setup works on all speed types.
- **Location matters:** Keep your defense villages close to the capital for faster troop movement.

	- On Annual Special worlds where clustering isn’t always possible, aim to keep 4-village clusters in nearby regions.
- **Start training early:** You don’t need every upgrade before training troops.

	- Many players start with Barracks and Stables level 20, then add infrastructure and Smithy upgrades gradually.
- **After upgrades:** Once your Smithy, Academy, or Hero’s Mansion have done their job, demolish them to make room for more **Warehouse or Granary** space.
- **Set up trade routes:** Automate supply deliveries from your cluster villages to your main defense base — it saves time and keeps queues running.

---

### Summary

Balancing your **economy and military** is key to becoming an effective defender.

To succeed:

- Develop your economy first, then scale military steadily.
- Use a **4-village cluster** to keep training running nonstop.
- Keep your defense infrastructure compact and efficient.
- Use trade routes to automate resource flow.

A strong economy supports a strong army — and that’s the foundation of every great defense account in *Travian: Legends*.
