# Travian Tournament - Game Worlds Setup

> Source: Travian: Legends Support  
> URL: https://support.travian.com/en/articles/108-travian-tournament-game-worlds-setup

---

All Travian Tournament game worlds follow the same setup, whether you are playing in a regional Qualification round or the Finals. The core mechanics remain consistent, although features may evolve over time as the game is updated. Always check official news for changes or newly activated features.

### Main Features

Tournament servers use standard Travian: Legends gameplay. This means:

- **No Annual Special mechanics** such as regions, Ancient Powers, or Victory Points.
- The map is the **regular Travian map**, not the Europe map.
- **Artefacts** and **Wonders of the World** are included.
- **Speed:** x2
- **Tribes:** All five tribes — Gauls, Teutons, Romans, Egyptians, and Huns—are available on every Tournament world.
- **Winning condition:** Upgrade a Wonder of the World to **level 100**.

### Feature Limitations

Some functions available on regular game worlds are disabled on Tournament servers:

- **No Vacation Mode**

	- You cannot activate Vacation Mode. Tournament play does not pause.
- **No Refer-a-Friend**

	- The Refer-a-Friend system is disabled. Tournament worlds are intended for competitive and experienced players.

### Gold Transfer Rules

- You may transfer Gold *to a Tournament avatar* only by using a **Gold Transfer Link from a Tournament game world** (from editions 2021 or newer).
- Gold transfer *from a Tournament avatar to other game worlds* has no special restrictions, except when the target world has its own limitations.

### Registration Rules

- Registration closes approximately **10 days after server start**.
- On Finals, registration is only possible **with a key**, which can be earned during the Qualification round.
