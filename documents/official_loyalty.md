# Loyalty

> Source: Travian: Legends Support  
> URL: https://support.travian.com/en/articles/66-loyalty

---

Every village in **Travian: Legends** starts with **100% loyalty**.
Loyalty determines how firmly a village belongs to your realm. When loyalty drops to **0%**, the village will switch allegiance and become part of the attacker’s realm.

---

### How Loyalty Works

- A village’s loyalty cannot decrease **as long as** it has one of the following buildings:

	- **Residence**
	- **Palace**
	- **Command Center**

If none of these buildings are present, loyalty can be reduced by **attacks with administrators** (Senators, Chiefs, Chieftains, Logades, Nomarchs, Ephors, or Jarls).
Once loyalty reaches **0%**, the village is conquered.

Learn more:

- [Conquering Villages](https://support.travian.com/articles/54)
- [Preventing Conquering](https://support.travian.com/articles/55)

---

### Increasing Loyalty

Loyalty automatically increases over time when a **Residence**, **Palace**, or **Command Center** exists in the village.

- Each building level increases loyalty by **2 points every 3 hours**, up to a maximum of **100%**.
- The **Tablet of Law** hero item can instantly increase loyalty by **1% per tablet**, up to **125%** total.

Learn more: [Hero Consumable Items](https://support.travian.com/articles/90)

---

### Decreasing Loyalty

Different tribes have different administrator strengths and random variations.
Each administrator reduces loyalty within the following ranges:

| **Tribe** | **Administrator** | **Loyalty Reduction** |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Romans | Senator | 20–30% |
| Teutons | Chief | 20–25% |
| Gauls | Chieftain | 20–25% |
| Huns | Logades | 15–30% |
| Egyptians | Nomarch | 20–25% |
| Spartans | Ephor | 20–25% |
| Vikings | Jarl | 15–30% |
| Viking Hero | – | 5% (cannot conquer villages alone) |

**Random Factor:**
The reduction amount has a random component that varies slightly between tribes.

- The **Huns** have the largest variation, making their results less predictable.
- When **multiple administrators** attack in the same wave, the random value is applied **once** for all of them — so it’s possible to see very high or low total results.

---

### Effects of Celebrations

**Attacker’s Great Celebration:**
If a **Great Celebration** is active in the attacker’s village, each administrator will reduce loyalty by an **additional 5%**.
Only applies to administrators from the celebrating village (not from others).
Also affects the **Viking Hero**.

**Defender’s Great Celebration:**
If the defending village is hosting a **Great Celebration**, each administrator reduces loyalty by **5% less**.
If both attacker and defender have Great Celebrations, the effects cancel out.
This also affects the **Viking Hero**.

---

### Removed Feature: Morale Bonus

The **morale bonus** that used to depend on village population differences has been **removed** and no longer applies.
