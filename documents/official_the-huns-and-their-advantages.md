# The Huns and Their Advantages

> Source: Travian: Legends Support  
> URL: https://support.travian.com/en/articles/7-the-huns-and-their-advantages

---

The **Huns** are a fierce, cavalry-focused tribe known for their speed, aggression, and devastating raids. Their mounted archers struck fear across the ancient world, and their entire society revolves around **military expansion** and **offensive dominance**.

Huns prefer to fight on horseback — first **raining arrows** on their enemies, then finishing them off with a **powerful charge**.
Their strength lies in mobility and attack power, but their **defenses are weak**, making cooperation with allies essential.

> The Huns are best suited for **experienced Travian players** who enjoy fast-paced raiding and coordinated offense. This tribe is **available only on 5-tribe servers**.

---

### Playstyle Tips

- Focus on **offense and raiding** — the Huns thrive on quick strikes and resource raids.
- Maintain **strong alliances** for defense; their units are not designed for holding ground.
- Use your **speed advantage** to outmaneuver slower opponents and control the map early.

---

### Special Features

| **Cavalry Power** | The Huns have **fast and powerful mounted units**, ideal for aggressive tactics. |
| --- | --- |
| **Makeshift Wall** | Provides only **weak defensive bonuses**, so it should not be relied upon. |
| **Defensive Limitations** | The tribe lacks strong defensive troops — rely on allies for protection. |
| **Merchants** | Can carry **500 resources** (speed: **20 fields/hour**). |
| **Logades** | The Huns’ administrator; lowers enemy village **loyalty by 15–30%**. |
| **Special Building – Command Center** | Expands your **settlement and expansion options**. |
| **Hero Bonus** | When mounted, your hero adds **+3 speed** to mounted armies (bonus scales with gameworld speed). |

---

### Strategy Summary

**Strengths:** Lightning-fast cavalry, exceptional offensive capability, strong raiding potential.
**Weaknesses:** Poor defense, weak wall, reliance on teamwork for protection.

> If you like to **strike fast, raid hard, and rely on mobility**, the Huns are your perfect match.
