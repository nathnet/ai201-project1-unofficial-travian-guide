# Hero Inventory - What to Equip and When

> Source: Travian: Legends Support  
> URL: https://support.travian.com/en/articles/170-hero-inventory-what-to-equip-and-when

---

## Item tiers & timing (know when to buy)

- Items come in **Tier 1 → Tier 2 → Tier 3** (same item, bigger bonus).
- On faster worlds, higher tiers appear **earlier**; prices usually spike at release, then drop—waiting can save silver. (See the timing table by server speed in the original.)

---

## Early game attacker & oasis farmer (fast XP, low risk)

Equip:

- **Helmet of Awareness** (more XP → faster leveling).
- **Light Segmented/Light Scale Armor** (damage reduction > flat Strength early).
- **Small Spurs** (mounted hero speed) + **Small Map** (faster returns).
- **Right-hand weapon** for your main attacking unit.
- **Horse** for speed.
- **Bandages** when escorting with **cavalry** (saves expensive losses).

---

## Mid → end game attacker (ops start)

- Keep the **same core set**; upgrade to **Tier 2/3** when affordable.
- Prefer **Spurs** over army-speed boots to avoid revealing a higher march speed to enemies.

---

## Mid–late game defender (arrive on time, survive)

Equip:

- **Helmet of Awareness** (XP still matters for bonuses).
- **Segmented/Scale/Breastplate Armor** (survivability).
- **Boots of the Warrior** (faster defensive moves). Keep **Spurs** too if you field lots of cav defense.
- **Standard** (faster travel between ally villages) and **Pennant** (own villages).
- **Defensive weapon** for the unit you send most (e.g., Pike for Spearmen; add others you actually field).
- **Horse** for speed; **Ointments** if many waves are expected.

---

## "I’m home" set (peace time)

- **Gladiator Helmet** (culture points).
- Optional **regen** pieces if you need passive healing.
Everything else can stay standard.

---

## Always-use consumables & utility (keep a stash)

- **Emergency resources** (feed reinforcements, rebuild Wall/Residence, etc.).
- **Ointments** (heal after fights).
- **Bandages** (best value on cav missions).
- **Tablets of Law** (instant loyalty bump when targeted).
- **Cages** (early game: clear/conquer oases; catch strong pets for defense).
- **Water Bucket** (revive hero fast).
- **Books of Wisdom** (respec for attack/defense; only needed until hero lvl 100).
- **Scrolls** (finish a level to full-heal).
- **Training helmets** (speed infantry/cavalry queues), **Helmet of Awareness**, **Gladiator Helmet**.
- **Any left-hand items + speed boots** that **reduce travel time** for your role.

---

## Defender pro-tip: carry a boot & banner toolkit

Defenders often need to **cut waves** or land **as close as possible** to incoming cats. Keep multiple combos and pick what fits the moment:

- Slowest move (quick return): **Map + Boots of Regeneration**.
- Fastest long move: **Boots of the Archon + Great Standard**.
Mix, test, and calculate to nail timings.
