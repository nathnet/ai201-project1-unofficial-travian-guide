# World Wonder

> Source: Travian: Legends Support  
> URL: https://support.travian.com/en/articles/103-world-wonder

---

The **World Wonder (WW)** is the ultimate building in Travian: Legends — and the key to winning the game world. The first player or alliance to build their World Wonder to **level 100** wins the server.

---

### The Purpose of the World Wonder

Each level of the World Wonder requires **hundreds of thousands — even millions — of resources**. Because of this enormous demand for materials and defense, it’s impossible for a single player to complete one alone. Usually, **entire alliances and their confederates** work together to collect resources, defend the World Wonder, and manage logistics. Building and maintaining a World Wonder is the most challenging and coordinated task in the game.

Learn about related items here: [Construction Plans](https://support.travian.com/articles/104)

---

### World Wonder Villages

From the start of every game world, **the Natars** hold several key villages — including 14 special **World Wonder villages**:

- 8 villages **outside the grey area**, each with **50% more defending troops**
- 6 villages **inside the grey area** in the **center of the map**
- The **Natar capital** always appears at coordinates **0|0**
- Other Natarian villages may appear later, as deleted player villages are converted to Natar control

Any of these **World Wonder villages** (except the Natar capital) can be **conquered by players** and used to **construct a World Wonder**.

---

### Important Rules for World Wonder Villages

World Wonder villages follow **special rules** that differ from normal ones:

- **No Walls or Residences/Palaces** – These buildings cannot be constructed.
- **No Gold Usage** – Gold cannot be used for any actions in this village (no NPC trade, no instant build).
- **Two Levels Queuable** – You may queue up to **two World Wonder levels** at a time.
- **No Cultural Points** – The village does not generate cultural points.
- **Faster Construction** – All building times are **50% shorter** than normal.
- **Reduced Crop Consumption** – After construction plans appear, troops in the World Wonder village consume **only 50% crop**.
- **Cannot Be Conquered** – Once the World Wonder reaches **level 1**, the village can **no longer be taken** by other players.

---

### Server End Conditions

The server ends when **one of the following happens**:

1. A **player’s World Wonder** reaches **level 100**, or
2. The **Natars** complete their **own World Wonder** at level 100.

---

### The Natar World Wonder

The Natars begin constructing their own World Wonder **a set number of days** after the server starts. If they reach **level 75**, all their remaining forces are recalled to defend their Wonder — meaning they will **no longer attack** player-controlled World Wonders from that point on.

---

### Construction Costs and Time

Each level of the World Wonder requires **huge amounts of resources and time**, depending on the game speed (1x, 2x, 3x, 5x, or 10x). Exact construction values are handled automatically by the system and can be checked directly in-game when managing your World Wonder village.

### Summary of Key Points

| Aspect | Description |
| --- | --- |
| **Main Goal** | Build a World Wonder to level 100 to win the server |
| **Who Can Build It** | Players or alliances that conquer a Natar World Wonder village |
| **Gold Use** | Not available (no NPC or instant build) |
| **Defense Requirement** | Extremely high; must be supported by the alliance |
| **Construction Time** | Half of normal village build times |
| **Crop Use** | 50% less after plans appear |
