# Quicklinks

> Source: Travian: Legends Support  
> URL: https://support.travian.com/en/articles/43-quicklinks

---

Quicklinks are customizable round buttons that appear in the **village details box** and in the **village list box** on the side of the game screen. They allow faster access to commonly used locations in your village.

This feature is only available on some gameworlds.
Editable quicklinks are available on **gameworlds starting from 08.01.2025**.

---

## **Village List Quicklinks**

- These buttons are available to **all players for free** and do **not** require Travian PLUS.
- The same customized set of **four** buttons appears in **all villages**.
- A **fifth button** is fixed and always leads to the **village overview**.
- You can customize which features the four adjustable buttons lead to.
- [Central Village Overview](https://support.travian.com/articles/42)

---

## **Village Details Quicklinks**

- The five quicklink buttons in the village details box require **active Travian PLUS**.
- If PLUS is inactive, these buttons appear **golden** to indicate they are locked.
- These quicklinks are **unique per village**, meaning each village can have its own set of buttons.

**Example:**
You might add a quicklink to the **Workshop** in villages where you produce catapults and rams, while in other villages you might link directly to the **Marketplace** or another frequently used building.

- [Travian Plus Membership](https://support.travian.com/articles/127)

---

## **Editing Quicklinks**

You can open the quicklink editing menu using the **three-dot button** at the end of the quicklink row.

- A pop-up will show all available quicklinks.
- You can **drag and drop** them into the desired slots.
- Quicklinks shown in **gold** require premium features such as Gold Club.
- **Greyed-out buttons** mean that the required building does not exist in the current village.
- You can remove buttons by dragging them out of the slot, and you may leave slots empty.

Available quicklinks include all troop training buildings, the Town Hall celebrations page, and various Marketplace and Rally Point tabs.

- [Gold Club](https://support.travian.com/articles/128)
