Welcome to the Game secrets guides. As request from the community we are giving a comparison table for all cavalry and infantry units parameters in Travian: Legends and [Northern Legends](https://blog.travian.com/travian-northern-legends/).

We hope that this table you can make informed decisions about which units to prioritize based on your strategy. Whether you’re focusing on offense, defense, or a balanced approach, understanding each unit’s efficiency will give you a competitive edge.

![](../assets/c41ceb32c7_MKT-3702-TL-AS24-TXT_DIVIDER_072024.png)
#### **Which parameters are used in the table:**

- **Attack Power**: The offensive strength of unit fully upgraded in Smithy.
- **Infantry Defense**: The unit’s ability (unit upgrade lvl 20) to defend against infantry attacks.
- **Cavalry Defense**: The unit’s ability (unit upgrade lvl 20) to defend against cavalry attacks.
- **Training time:** Given for the fully upgraded building.
- **Trained per 24h**: The number of units that can be trained in 24 hours at x1 speed, with level 20 barracks and stables, **in crop consumption**. For Romans the calculation also includes a level 20 Horse Drinking Trough (HDT). Alliance bonuses, helmets and artifacts are not taken into account.
- **Total Costs in 24h**: The total resources required to train these units non-stop for 24 hours in fully upgraded barracks or stable.
- **Attack, Defense against Infantry and Defense Against Cavalry Points in 24h**: This includes Attack, Infantry Defense or Cavalry Defense, assuming full smithy upgrades and continuous training at full-level barracks or stables (plus HDT for Romans), without any other bonuses.
- **Legionnaire (new)** The new parameters of legionnaire that is currently present in Northern Legends scenario.
