# Access Keys

> Source: Travian: Legends Support  
> URL: https://support.travian.com/en/articles/158-access-keys

---

Access keys let you jump straight to common in-game pages using your keyboard—no mouse needed. Most browsers support this with a simple key combo (often **Alt + access key** or **Ctrl + access key**; exact combo varies by browser). If those don’t work, check your browser’s access-key shortcut.

---

## How to use

1. Hold your browser’s modifier (usually **Alt** or **Ctrl**).
2. Press the access key from the list below.
You’ll be taken directly to the corresponding page.

---

## Access key list

- **1** – Resource view
- **2** – Village centre
- **3** – Map
- **4** – Statistics
- **5** – Reports
- **6** – In-game messages
- **7** – Daily quests
- **8** – Gold Shop
- **9** – Village overview
- **b** – Previous village in your village list
- **n** – Next village in your village list

---

## Tips

- If nothing happens, try the other common modifier (switch **Alt** ![left_right_arrow emoji](../assets/cb9fe3016e_2194-fe0f.png) **Ctrl**) and ensure the game tab is focused.
- Keep this list handy to speed up routine tasks like reading reports or hopping between villages.
