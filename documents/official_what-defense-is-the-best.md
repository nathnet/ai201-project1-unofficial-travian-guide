# What Defense is the best?

> Source: Travian: Legends Support  
> URL: https://support.travian.com/en/articles/180-what-defense-is-the-best

---

There is no universal “best” defensive unit or strategy in Travian: Legends. The right choice depends on your account’s development, available resources, tribe, gameworld conditions, and your role in the alliance. This guide outlines common factors that influence which defensive units work best for different situations.

---

## Choosing the Best Defense for Your Situation

### **1. High Production, Plenty of Gold, and Located Far Away?**

Choose **fast cavalry defense**.
Fast-moving defensive cavalry helps you reach distant allies quickly and react to emergencies across the map.

### **2. Low on Gold and Resources?**

Choose **cheap infantry**.
Infantry generally offers the highest **defense per resource cost**, making it ideal for players who want to contribute without heavy spending.

### **3. Expecting Intense Battles Soon?**

Choose units with the best **defense/time** efficiency.
In heavy combat situations, troop speed and how quickly you can produce defense matter more than crop consumption.

### **4. Preparing for World Wonder Defense or Low Crop Production?**

Choose units with strong **crop efficiency**.
Long-term defense—especially for World Wonder stages—often depends on low crop consumption to sustain large troop numbers.

### **5. Prefer Building Fewer Defense Villages?**

Choose a tribe whose units allow **infantry + cavalry** production in the same village.
This allows you to maintain flexibility without spreading production across many villages.

## Overview of Defensive Units

Below is a summary of the main defensive units and their performance.
The table reflects production on a **x1 server**, assuming **level 20 barracks and/or stables**, no bonuses, and training for one full week (~168 hours). It includes:

- Number of units produced
- Defensive strength vs. infantry
- Defensive strength vs. cavalry
- Total defense output
- Speed and upkeep
- Hourly cost
- Defense/time efficiency
- Defense/cost efficiency

This helps you compare units based on what you value most: speed, cost, output, or sustainability.

**Examples from the table:**

- **Phalanxes** offer an excellent balance of cost and total defense with strong efficiency.
- **Spearmen** have great defense per training time and strong cavalry protection.
- **Praetorians** are the strongest infantry defense per unit.
- **Slave Militia** provide the best defense per resource cost, but at the expense of crop and flexibility.
- **Druidriders** and **Elpida Riders** offer fast reaction speed but lower cost-efficiency.
- **Haeduans** deliver high cavalry defense but are expensive to maintain.

Each unit excels for different reasons, and the best choice depends on your goals and your account’s economy.

---

## Summary

The “best” defensive units are those that match your account’s strengths and your alliance’s needs. Consider:

- Your crop production
- Resource availability
- Distance to allies
- Expected battle intensity
- Your tribe’s unique strengths

Use the defense/time and defense/cost metrics to determine whether you should prioritize fast reinforcements, cheap mass defense, or high-quality defensive power.
