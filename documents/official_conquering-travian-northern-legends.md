# Conquering Travian: Northern Legends

> Source: Travian: Legends Support  
> URL: https://support.travian.com/en/articles/200-conquering-travian-northern-legends

---

*Northern Legends* is a special annual Travian: Legends scenario that introduces unique mechanics, region control, and new strategic opportunities. Success here depends on adaptability, resource planning, and strong teamwork. This guide highlights key tips to help you and your alliance navigate this scenario effectively.

---

## Early Game Tips

### **Use the Respawn Feature Wisely**

If your starting location is weak, unsafe, or far from strategic regions, respawn early.
This lets you reposition without losing much progress.

**Important:** Non-refundable gold (NPC trades, instant completes, etc.) is lost when respawning, so spend gold carefully.
More info: Respawn Feature Guide.

---

### **Optimize Your Resources Early**

Upgrade your fields and resource-boosting buildings early.
Strong production ensures:

- Faster army growth
- Better early expansion
- More flexibility for settling or supporting alliance operations

---

### **Settle Near Mountain Chains for a Strong Resource Base**

The Alps, Carpathians, and similar mountain ranges offer many **iron and iron-crop oases**, making them great for early farming. Since hero inventory resources are important for early region capture, this is especially valuable.

If your gameworld lets you choose tribes for first settlements, consider settling a **Hun village** around oasis-rich regions for excellent income.

---

### **Use a Universal Harbor Early**

You can build:

- **Warships** for sending reinforcements quickly
- **Raid ships** for distant oasis farming
- **Trade ships** to support your economy

You can change specialization later—just demolish unneeded ships and build new ones.

---

### **Find an Alliance by Settling Smart**

If you enter the world without a premade team, settle in regions with **valuable early powers** (e.g., small boots or eyes).
This increases your chance of joining a strong alliance early, improving your long-term stability.

---

## Military Development

### **Diversify Your Playstyle**

Relying on just offense or just defense is rarely optimal in Northern Legends.
A **hybrid approach**, with both offensive and defensive troops, gives you flexibility and helps your alliance react to changing threats.

---

### **Focus on Operational (Working) Hammers**

Northern Legends does **not** include World Wonders, so alliances don’t need massive WW hammers.

Instead, fast-trained, mid-sized **operational hammers** are preferred:

- Easy to rebuild
- Effective for regional fights
- Suitable for frequent clearing and conquering

---

## Strategic Expansion & Regional Control

### **Prioritize Culture Points and Production**

Regions in Northern Legends are held by **population**, and taking new regions requires enough **Culture Points (CP)**.

To stay competitive:

- Build strong resource villages
- Invest in CP-generating buildings
- Develop villages steadily instead of rushing too far too fast

---

### **Avoid Overextending**

Fast expansion can leave you vulnerable.
Before pushing into new regions:

- Ensure your defenses are stable
- Train enough troops to protect your cluster
- Build villages in **clusters of 3–4** to use defense forwarding efficiently

---

### **Watch for Regional Power Shifts**

Region control can change quickly.
Monitor the map regularly to:

- Defend threatened regions
- Identify weak rivals
- Push into regions where enemies just lost control

---

### **Check Rankings Regularly**

Tracking player and alliance rankings helps you:

- Identify rising threats
- Spot weakening opponents
- Plan resource investments
- Choose when to strike or reinforce

---

### **Stay Adaptable**

Northern Legends evolves as alliances shift and regions change hands.
Strategies that work early may fail later—adjust your approach as the meta develops.

---

## Alliance Coordination

In Northern Legends, **teamwork decides everything**.
Coordinate with your alliance on:

- Where to settle
- Which regions to target
- When to launch operations
- Reinforcement plans
- Harbor and naval movements

A well-coordinated alliance can dominate entire regions with fewer players than an uncoordinated group.

---

## Summary

To succeed in Northern Legends:

- Use respawn smartly
- Build a strong early economy
- Take advantage of oasis-rich regions
- Balance offense and defense
- Prioritize CP and growth
- Expand carefully and react quickly
- Maintain constant alliance coordination

Mastering these principles will give you the flexibility and strength needed to thrive in Travian’s most dynamic annual scenario.
