# Beginner's Protection

> Source: Travian: Legends Support  
> URL: https://support.travian.com/en/articles/12-beginner-s-protection

---

When you start Travian: Legends, your village is placed under **Beginner’s Protection**. This gives you a safe window to learn the game, build up resources, and prepare your defenses before facing real attacks.

---

## How Beginner’s Protection Works

- **No troop interactions:** While protected, no one can attack, raid, scout, or send reinforcements to you — and you cannot send troops to others either.
- **Safe trading:** You cannot send resources, but you *can* receive them from players who are no longer under protection.
- **Marketplace rules:**

	- If you have **under 200 population**, you can only accept trades that are 1:1 or better.
	- You can still post worse trades, but no one can accept them — useful as **extra storage space**.
- **Attacking exceptions:** You *can* attack Natars and unoccupied oases even while under protection.
- **No early exit:** Protection cannot be ended early.

---

## Extending Your Protection

- As your protection nears its end, you’ll see an **infobox message** offering an extension.
- You can extend protection **once**.
- Once protection ends, it’s gone permanently — it cannot be regained.

---

## Duration of Beginner’s Protection

The length depends on the gameworld speed:

- **x1 speed:** 5 days + 3-day extension
- **x2 speed:** 3 days + 3-day extension
- **x3 speed:** 3 days + 3-day extension
- **x5 speed:** 2 days + 2-day extension
- **x10 speed:** 1 day + 1-day extension

---

**Tip:** Use Beginner’s Protection to focus on upgrading your economy, completing quests, and joining an alliance. That way, once it ends, you’ll be ready to defend yourself.
