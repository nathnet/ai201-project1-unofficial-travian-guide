# Advanced Start – Fast Fourth Village (x2 Speed)

> Source: Travian: Legends Support  
> URL: https://support.travian.com/en/articles/202-advanced-start-fast-fourth-village-x2-speed

---

Gameworlds with **Advanced Start** are becoming more frequent in Travian: Legends. This feature speeds up early development and reduces the time before battles begin—but it also increases the importance of planning your economy from the very first minutes. Your main early-game goal in an Advanced Start world is usually simple.

### **Settle your fourth village (your future capital) as fast as possible.**

This guide offers a clear, practical path to reach the fourth village quickly on **x2 speed** servers.
Feel free to adjust based on your tribe, playstyle, or experience.

---

## General First Steps

### **Step 1 – Activate Bonuses**

Immediately enable your **+25% resource production bonuses** after registration.

### **Step 2 – Run Adventures**

Send your hero to the first adventure right away.
As your hero returns:

- Complete every adventure as soon as possible.
- Assign **all points into resource production**.
- If you spawned near strong oasis clusters, you may optionally hunt oases first and switch points using the **Book of Wisdom** once you obtain it from the 6th adventure.

### **Step 3 – Settle Your 2nd and 3rd Villages**

Send your two settler groups immediately.
You may:

- Settle both villages close to your spawn (safer and faster CP)
- Or travel further toward a future cropper (shorter distance later but slower CP now)

A longer walking time delays Culture Points (CP), so choose balancing speed vs. long-term positioning.

---

## Setup Used in This Guide

This route assumes:

- You start as **Egyptians** (best early economy)
- All **hero points go into resources**
- **No oasis farming**
- Villages 2 and 3 are settled **close to your spawn**
- If your world allows **“select second village tribe”**, use it for flexibility

Keep your hero alive—hero production is critical for hitting CP goals fast.

When a line says “Main Building 12,” it doesn’t mean building it immediately.
Instead:

- Prioritize **cheaper CP buildings** when online
- Build expensive buildings during **offline time**
- Always maintain the overall order of development

---

## Goal Breakdown

To settle village 4, you must:

1. **Reach 1,525 Culture Points**, and
2. **Accumulate enough resources** for:

	- Two small celebrations
	- Residence level 10 (in one settled village)
	- Training three settlers
	- Sending them to the new location

## Final Steps Before Settling the Fourth Village

### **In Your Settler Village (only one village):**

1. Upgrade **Residence from level 2 to level 10**
2. Train **3 settlers** (total cost: 63,000 resources)

### **Celebrations:**

- Run small celebrations in both villages at ~150 CP each (22,360 resources per celebration)

### **Send Settlers**

Once CP and resources are prepared, send your settlers to claim your fourth village — ideally a high-bonus cropper.

## Summary

To settle your fourth village quickly on an x2 Advanced Start world:

- Activate bonuses immediately
- Maximize hero resource production
- Settle villages 2 and 3 early and efficiently
- Build high-CP buildings in the right order
- Run early celebrations
- Push Residence 10 in one village
- Train settlers and move fast toward your chosen cropper

With proper execution, reaching the fourth village in **36–40 hours** is fully achievable—even without oasis farming.
