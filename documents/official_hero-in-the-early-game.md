# Hero in the early game

> Source: Travian: Legends Support  
> URL: https://support.travian.com/en/articles/141-hero-in-the-early-game

---

Your hero plays a vital role in your early game progress — both for your economy and for securing your future capital. Managing your hero well can make the difference between a fast start and falling behind.

---

### Why Your Hero Matters

In the early game, every hour counts. Losing your hero in an adventure or risky oasis raid can set you back for hours and delay your second village. Because many players assign all their hero points into resource production at the start, keeping the hero alive is even more important.

If you want to secure a valuable **cropper** (a 15-crop village for your capital), make sure you follow an established development guide and manage your hero carefully.

---

### Hero Resource Production

| Tribe | Resource Production per Point (x1 speed) | If Focused on One Resource | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| All (except Egyptians) | 9 resources of each type | 30 of the selected resource | +25% resource bonus applies |
| Egyptians | 12 resources of each type | 40 of the selected resource | +25% resource bonus applies |

Your hero’s resource production increases with game speed (e.g. x2, x3 worlds). Don’t forget that the **25% hero production bonus** applies to these values too.

### Adventure Item Drops

The items your hero can find in adventures depend on how far you are into the game:

| Adventure Range | Item Availability |
| --- | --- |
| 1–31 | No scrolls, no small or large bandages, no artworks |
| 32–61 | No large bandages |
| 62+ | All items possible |

> **Note:** On Annual Special worlds, small bandages can rarely appear before adventure 31 (if the hero “switches” from finding a Natar Horn to bandages instead).

### Key Tips for the Early Game Hero

- Keep your hero alive — avoid sending them into dangerous adventures without ointments or enough health.
- Prioritize **resource production** points early on, especially if your goal is a fast second village.
- Use **safe adventures** first (low health loss) and collect early rewards like silver, ointments, and the horse.
- Remember: every minute your hero is dead is lost production time.

---

### Summary

Your hero is not just a fighter — they’re the backbone of your early economy.
To progress quickly:

- Distribute points smartly into resource production.
- Manage adventures carefully to avoid death.
- Collect useful early rewards.
- Use the gathered silver and items strategically.

Taking care of your hero means taking care of your entire account’s development — especially when aiming for a fast and efficient start in **Travian: Legends**.
