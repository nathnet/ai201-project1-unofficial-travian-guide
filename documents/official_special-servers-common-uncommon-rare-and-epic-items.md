# Special Servers - Common, Uncommon, Rare and Epic Items

> Source: Travian: Legends Support  
> URL: https://support.travian.com/en/articles/23-special-servers-common-uncommon-rare-and-epic-items

---

This article lists all equipment items available in **Reign of Fire**, along with their bonus values for every rarity level. Items can have their rarity improved through **refining**, and completely new items of varying rarity can be created through **forging**.

> [Special Servers - Item Crafting](https://support.travian.com/support/solutions/articles/7000094922)

Every item still has a **quality tier**, which **cannot be changed**.

To help you find the details you need, the items are organized into separate tables for armour, helmets, left-hand items, right-hand items, and boots.

Because there are many items, and the tables are long, it is recommended to use your browser’s search function (for example, **Ctrl+F** on Windows).

Please note: Some states and numbers might change due to balancing updates and adjustments.
