# Hero Experience

> Source: Travian: Legends Support  
> URL: https://support.travian.com/en/articles/49-hero-experience

---

Your **hero gains experience (XP)** through **battles**, **adventures**, **daily quests**, **tasks**, and some **items**.
When enough XP is collected, your hero **levels up** and receives **4 additional attribute points** to spend.

---

## How to Earn Hero XP

You can gain hero experience from:

- **Battles** (attacking or defending)
- [Adventures](https://support.travian.com/articles/46)
- [Daily Quests](https://support.travian.com/articles/16)
- [Task System](https://support.travian.com/articles/17)
- [Hero Items](https://support.travian.com/articles/89)(specific consumables and equipment)

---

## Leveling Up

Each time your hero reaches the XP threshold for the next level:

- You gain **4 new attribute points** to assign.
- Your hero is **fully healed automatically**.

- [Hero Overview](https://support.travian.com/articles/45)

---

## Gaining XP in Battles

The amount of XP gained from combat depends directly on **enemy troop losses**.
Each **1 crop consumption point** of a killed enemy unit grants **1 XP** to your hero.

---

### Attacking Battles

- If your hero **participates in the attack**, they gain XP equal to the total **supply value** of all enemy units killed.
- You gain this XP even if:

	- The **battle is lost**, or
	- Your **hero dies** in combat.

---

### Defensive Battles

Defensive XP is divided among all defending heroes based on the **proportion of their armies’ total crop consumption** compared to the total defense. This system is similar to how **defense ranking points** are distributed.

---

### Example 1 – All Heroes Only

If multiple heroes defend without other troops:

> Situation: Player A attacks with 10 Theutates Thunders and 10 Phalanxes (total 30 crop supply).
> 10 defending heroes each have 6 crop supply.
> Each hero receives **10%** of the total XP (3 XP each).

---

### Example 2 – Mixed Defense

If some defenders send additional troops, XP is split unevenly:

> Situation: Same attack (30 crop supply).
>
> - Hero 1 defends **alone** (6 crop supply).
> - Hero 2 defends with **88 Phalanxes** (total 100 crop supply).
>
> Result:
>
> - Hero 1 gets **6% XP (2 XP)**.
> - Hero 2 gets **94% XP (28 XP)**.

Mathematical rounding applies to all XP results.
