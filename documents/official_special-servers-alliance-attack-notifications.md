# Special Servers - Alliance Attack Notifications

> Source: Travian: Legends Support  
> URL: https://support.travian.com/en/articles/31-special-servers-alliance-attack-notifications

---

On some **special servers**, the **Alliance Attack Notifications** feature is enabled.
If active, it will always be **announced before the server starts**.

---

## How It Works

This feature displays **incoming attack and raid notifications** for the entire **alliance** — even if a player has marked the attack as “green” (acknowledged) in their Rally Point.

- The **Alliance Member list** shows only **attacks and raids** where the **number of incoming troops** is **greater than the Rally Point level** of the target village.

	- Example: If the Rally Point is **level 20**, any attack or raid with **fewer than 20 units** will not appear on the alliance overview.
- The number of **attacks** and **raids** is shown **separately** for clarity.

---

## Important Notes

- The **Eagle Eyes** Ancient Power **does not affect** what is visible in these notifications.
- This feature improves alliance coordination by allowing leaders and defenders to see large-scale threats quickly.

---

**Tip:** Keep an eye on your **Alliance → Members tab** when this feature is active — it’s a great way to spot coordinated attacks early and organize a defense faster.
