# The Vikings and Their Advantages

> Source: Travian: Legends Support  
> URL: https://support.travian.com/en/articles/4-the-vikings-and-their-advantages

---

### Overview

The **Vikings** are fierce warriors and legendary explorers, known for their dominance across land and sea. With a long history of **conquest and raiding**, they bring a unique set of features and tactics to *Travian: Legends*.

Masters of seafaring, the Vikings can construct the **fastest raid ships** in the game, allowing them to strike swiftly and unpredictably. Their armies rely primarily on **powerful infantry** supported by **efficient cavalry**, making them formidable offensive players.

> The Vikings are designed for **experienced players** who enjoy **aggressive raiding**, **strategic offense**, and a mix of land and naval combat.

---

### Playstyle Tips

- Build your strategy around **aggressive raiding** — Viking infantry hits hard and excels at offense.
- Make the most of the **fast raid ships** to reach targets others can’t.
- Use your **Valkyrie’s Blessing** cavalry for efficient, low-crop support in mixed attacks.
- Manage your **crop usage** carefully — **Berserkers** consume double crop but can devastate enemies when unleashed.
- Keep a balance between infantry and cavalry to stay flexible in both short and long campaigns.

---

### Special Features

| FeatureDescription |
| --- |
| **Infantry Strength** | Extremely strong infantry, especially **Berserkers**, but **expensive to train and maintain**. |
| **Cavalry Efficiency** | **Valkyrie’s Blessing** units are heavy cavalry that use only **2 crop supply**, lower than typical heavy cavalry. |
| **Barricade** | Provides a **small defensive bonus** — limited but useful in early defense. |
| **Merchants** | Can carry **750 resources** at a speed of **18 fields/hour**. |
| **Jarl** | Lowers enemy village **loyalty by 15–30%**. |
| **Special Building – Harbor** | A **better harbor** that produces **faster raid ships** (24 fields/hour compared to the standard 18). |
| **Hero Bonus** | The Viking hero can **lower enemy village loyalty by 5%** in both **attacks and raids**. [Hero Overview](https://support.travian.com/en/support/solutions/articles/7000061665) |

---

### Strategy Summary

**Strengths:** Powerful infantry, fast and efficient raid ships, strong offensive potential.
**Weaknesses:** High troop cost and crop consumption, weak defenses.

> If you thrive on **relentless raiding**, **high-speed attacks**, and **commanding power on both land and sea**, the Vikings are your ultimate choice.
